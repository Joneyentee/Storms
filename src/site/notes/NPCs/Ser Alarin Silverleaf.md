### Description

A knight or guard who responded to the town square ambush in [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]] during Session 22. Silverleaf fought alongside [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] and [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]] to eliminate the assassins targeting [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] and [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]. Despite the similar name, he is a completely different person from [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]], the elf knight whose mansion was burned down.

### Key Details

- Responded to town square combat during Session 22
- Fought with long sword and javelin
- Killed multiple assassins during the battle
- Wielded both weapons interchangeably (can make two attacks using javelin or long sword in any combination)
- +6 to hit, deals 2d10+4 damage
