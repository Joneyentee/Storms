#### Description
Lady Sarafina Moonwhisper is a young elf (~200 years old) from the woodland elf tribes (Woodkin), cousin to [[NPCs/Gawain of the Woodkin\|Gawain of the Woodkin]]. Dragged to the tournament by family tradition despite being an outsider to court politics. Friendly and helpful to fellow outsiders.

#### Key Details
- Elf, approximately 200 years old (young for her race)
- Cousin to [[NPCs/Gawain of the Woodkin\|Gawain of the Woodkin]]
- From Woodkin tribe (woodland elves, like Dalish from Dragon Age)
- Dragged to tournament by family despite being outsider
- Tournament competitor - lost to [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] in Round 1 (unhorsed immediately)
- Gave jousting advice to [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] and [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]
- Friendly to party members
