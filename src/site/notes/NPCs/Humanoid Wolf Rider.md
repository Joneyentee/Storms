## Description

Female orc riding a giant Frost Wolf (Large creature). Spotted twice at a distance before engaging the party directly in Session 36. Combat-capable; carries throwing axes and wears a plate-brace bracer. Serves or answers to an individual named [[NPCs/Zorn\|Zorn]]. Species, full identity, and allegiance unknown.

## Key Details

- **Race:** Orc (female)
- **Mount:** Giant Frost Wolf (Large creature; dealt cold damage on attacks)
- **Equipment:** Throwing axes (dual), plate-brace bracer (used to parry)
- **Confirmed connection:** Serves/answers to [[NPCs/Zorn\|Zorn]] (named S36)
- **Threat level:** Confirmed hostile; capable fighter
- **Status:** Withdrew voluntarily after warning party about Zorn
