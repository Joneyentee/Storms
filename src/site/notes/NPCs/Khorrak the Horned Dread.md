**Faction:** Antagonist | **Status:** DEFEATED (Session 30) **Race:** Minotaur | **Class:** Warrior (statue guardian) **Tags:** #npc/antagonist #npc/major-threat #npc/ancient-evil #npc/dream-realm #npc/deceased

### Description

Monstrous-sized minotaur in full armour, towering over party members. Immortalised as statue in Hall of Statues, depicted mid-leap with massive hammer downward, muscles taut and powerful. Inscription reads: "To see the Horned Dread charge was to know the end." Awakened when party collected first gem from dragon statue in Dream Temple labyrinth (Session 29). **Defeated Session 30** in two-phase boss battle - body disintegrated into green ooze that seeped under rock transforming [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]].

### Combat Mechanics (Session 30)

**Phase 1 - Initial Form:**

- Monstrous-sized (large category) - cannot be surrounded for advantage
- Black fur, heavy armour, wielded massive hammer and knife
- Can break through stone walls at will
- Tracks by sound and scent
- Immune to being frightened
- **Killed by [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]'s Guiding Bolt** (18 radiant damage)

**Phase 2 - Enraged Resurrection:**

- Eerie green glow appeared throughout body after "death"
- Hand clutched chest, pulled wound closed healing completely
- Black fur turned glowing green
- Roared and became enraged
- **Target locked:** [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] (whoever dealt Phase 1 killing blow)
- Advantage on all attack rolls
- Single-minded focus, immune to stunning
- **Legendary Action:** "Minotaur's Recall" - teleport back along travelled path without using movement
- **Killed by [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s Fire Bolt** (14 fire damage)

**Death:**

- Erupted into flames (orangey amber turning green)
- Disintegrated into green liquid
- Green ooze mysteriously disappeared from floor
- Seeped under rock crushing [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]
- Ooze covered her body triggering Warg ability transformation

### Key Details

- **Pursuit duration:** Chased party through six dragon chambers across Session 29
- **Wall breaking:** Smashed through two walls to cut off escape routes
- **Combat damage taken:** Approximately 200+ total damage across both phases
- **Key vulnerabilities:** Acid damage effective ([[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]'s Chromatic Orb), bludgeoning damage ([[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]]'s unarmed strikes)
- **Stunning Strike:** Worked in Phase 1 ([[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] stunned for one round), failed in Phase 2 (enraged immunity)
- **Critical hits:** Natural 20 on [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] (22 slashing damage forcing her to 0 HP)

### Transformation Trigger

**[[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]'s Warg Ability:**

- Crushed under debris when Khorrak crashed through wall
- Remained unconscious entire battle
- After party killed Khorrak, green ooze disappeared from floor
- Ooze seeped under rock covering her body
- When party lifted debris, body whisped away in green smoke
- Transformation granted Warg ability
- DM: "Left her too long under slab" + "killed minotaur next to it"
