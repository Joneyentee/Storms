## Description

One of 16 knights under party command. Known as "the Stalwart" for his absolute discipline and iron will. Knight of iron will born to command - leadership style is one of absolute discipline where every decision is calculated. When he speaks, his voice is a hammer: unyielding, yet with rhythm. His men know exactly what is expected of them. Where others rely on force or wisdom, he relies on pure discipline and calculated strategy. Currently candidate for Sylobelle Steward with strong recommendation from [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]].
