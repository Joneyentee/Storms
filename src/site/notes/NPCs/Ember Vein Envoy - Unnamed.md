### Description

A woman of unknown race and origin encountered in the ritual chamber of the [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] underground complex beneath [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]]. She wears a black and red cloak consistent with [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] livery, but her bearing, abilities, and apparent agenda set her apart from the cult's rank and file. Her voice carries an otherworldly quality — soft, distant, like wind heard from far away — and her eyes shimmer with visible concern when she speaks directly to the party.

### Key Details

- Largely immune to physical attacks; [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s longsword passed clean through her twice as though she were not fully present in the material world
- Able to drop magical illusions covering entire walls and animate a petrified dragonborn statue through sustained incantation — lit four pedestals in sequence via a chanting ritual
- Teleported [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] from the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]] directly into the underground chamber
- Restored the entire party to full health, spell slots, and ki points with a snap of her fingers immediately before the boss encounter
- Addressed the party directly at session end, implying she has observed them across multiple separate incidents: _"Twice now I have seen you unravelling that which was never meant to wake"_ — suggesting the awakening of [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] and the stirring of the seraph are both known to her
- Speaks of the seraph and ancient entities with authority and clear prior knowledge — her stated goal appears to be containment, not awakening
- Relationship to the [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] hierarchy (Inner Ember, High Flame) is unclear; may be a superior rank or an entirely separate power

### Relationships

- Connected to: [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] (affiliation confirmed by livery; true rank unknown)
- Opposed (apparently): [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] (operated beneath her in the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]]; her broader agenda appears distinct from his)
- Animated: [[NPCs/Purple Dragonborn\|Purple Dragonborn]]
- Observed: [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] (claims to have watched them across at least two significant events)
