## Description

Worn, bookish/scholarly appearance with combed greying hair and rimmed glasses. Professor of ancient civilisations. Found mortally wounded in underground atrium beneath bandit castle near [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]], stabbed through with a sword. Died after revealing "They took the doll" - referring to the magical doll that would become [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]].

## Key Details

- Specialty: Ancient civilisations
- Physical: Worn appearance, greying hair, rimmed glasses
- Cover Story: Claimed to be searching for daughter "[[NPCs/Kendara Elley Aloison\|Kendara Elley Aloison]]" - this was actually code for the doll
- True Mission: Protecting/searching for magical doll (later transformed into [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]])
- Death: Found slumped by wall in underground atrium, stabbed through with sword, barely clinging to life
- Last Words: "They took the doll"
- **Revelation:** "Kendara" was never a real child - it was the doll's cover identity
