## Description

One of 16 knights under party command. Known as "of the Wildlands" for his background. The charismatic rebel - a tempest, not so much a knight of tradition but one who brings his own way to the battlefield. Where others see structure, he sees chaos, and he loves the chaos. His leadership is like the crackling of a fire: unpredictable, yet warm and inviting to those who follow him. Has a tendency to succeed despite unconventional methods. Not the sort of man to stand in a tent with a scroll and quill - he's usually on the front hand smacking someone in the face. His soldiers would follow him into any situation. Currently serves as [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s squire and knight. Candidate for Sylobelle Steward - popular with troops but considered risky for long-term governance.
