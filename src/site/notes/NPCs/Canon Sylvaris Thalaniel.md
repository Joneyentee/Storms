Serves as Keeper of the Sacred Archives at the Cathedral of Gentle Redemption in [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]]. Stood on the dais alongside [[NPCs/High Luminary Aelthir Vaelorian\|High Luminary Aelthir Vaelorian]] during the morning service. Allegiance unknown.
