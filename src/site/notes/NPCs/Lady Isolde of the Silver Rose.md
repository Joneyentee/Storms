## Description

One of 16 knights under party command. Master tactician who may not be as thunderous in command as Ser Oldric, but her mind is a blade sharper than any sword. Where Oldric relies on force, she relies on wisdom. She is a master tactician who often sees the broader picture, leading her troops like pieces on a chessboard rather than as people. **Appointed mayor of [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] (Session 31).**
