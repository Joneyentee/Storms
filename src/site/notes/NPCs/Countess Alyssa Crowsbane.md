## Description

Lady Alyssa Crowsbane, Countess of [[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]], is a centuries-old elf who maintains a youthful appearance and the vigour to compete in the Grand Tournament. Beautiful and deceptive, she initially appeared to have a soft spot for [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]], which turned out to be an elaborate ploy. She imprisoned [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] in her tower before the tournament.
