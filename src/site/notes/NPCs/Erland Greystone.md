## Description

Village elder of [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]]. Works alongside newly appointed mayor [[NPCs/Lady Isolde of the Silver Rose\|Lady Isolde of the Silver Rose]] as local authority figure. Investigated [[NPCs/Jon Doe\|Jon Doe]] murder and coordinated with [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] during murder investigation. Suspected [[NPCs/Seraphine\|Seraphine]] of foul play due to her being new to village (suspicion proved incorrect).

## Investigation Details (Session 31)

**[[NPCs/Jon Doe\|Jon Doe]] Murder:**

- Victim: Nephew of previous mayor
- Death: Throat slit in three places
- Scene: House cracked and falling in, significant structural damage
- Timeline: Approximately one week before investigation
- Evidence: Large canine paw prints under split floorboards

**[[NPCs/Sofia\|Sofia]] Attempted Murder:**

- Victim: Local villager
- Attack: Stalked at night, ground shook, hit on back of head
- Witness: [[NPCs/Terry\|Terry]] (guard) shouted and gave chase
- Key detail: Attacker glimpsed with tail
- Timeline: Few nights before party investigation

**Suspect Theory:**

- Blamed [[NPCs/Seraphine\|Seraphine]] for murders
- Reasoning: New to village, manipulates elements, suspicious timing
- Bias: Classic "blame the outsider" prejudice
- Result: Theory disproven by party investigation
