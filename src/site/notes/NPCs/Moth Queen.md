## Description

Large moth-like creature that led moth swarms stealing potatoes from [[Locations/Crossroads Inn\|Crossroads Inn]]. Operated from [[Locations/Moth Queen Cavern\|Moth Queen Cavern]] ("The Cavern of the Fluttering Menace") with inscription "Glory to the Moth Queen" above entrance. Commanded six smaller moth minions (1ft wide). **Revealed to be transformed [[NPCs/Oswick Porridgepot\|Oswick Porridgepot]]** (halfling with crooked nose and missing right hand who disappeared 3 months ago searching for "mythical Garlic Patch"). Transformation method unknown, Bane influence suspected. Defeated by [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] Session 34.
