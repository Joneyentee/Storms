## Description

[[NPCs/Professor Gabriel Emms\|Professor Gabriel Emms]]'s scholarly friend who was entrusted with the magical doll (later became [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]]). Found murdered in farmhouse near [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]], pinned to wall with large spear/pike weapon. Left partial torn letter warning "He is coming. He knows about her."

## Key Details

- Full Name: Ryvenien Berg (from scroll found at scene)
- Profession: Scholar
- Relationship: Friend of [[NPCs/Professor Gabriel Emms\|Professor Gabriel Emms]]
- Mission: Entrusted with magical doll (cover story: Professor's "daughter" [[NPCs/Kendara Elley Aloison\|Kendara Elley Aloison]])
- Death: Murdered, pinned to wall with large spear/pike weapon
- Location: Farmhouse near [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]]
- Discovered: After raid on [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] (four nights after raid)

## Murder Scene

**Discovery:**

- Party searched farmhouse where Ryvenien and "Kendara" supposedly lived
- Found body pinned to wall with large spear/pike weapon
- Scroll addressed to "Ryvenien Berg" found at scene
- Partial torn letter found (see below)

**The Partial Letter:** Torn and only partially readable:

> He is coming. He knows about her. Prayer... I've left a key in the sanctuary below where King ........... dwelled ........... beneath ..........

**Village Testimony:**

- Four nights prior: Raiding party from south attacked [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]]

- No villagers knew anyone named "Ryvenien"
- No one recognized description of "Kendara"
- Sheep, cattle, villagers killed
- Buildings destroyed/burned
