## Description

**Second son of High King Everglade**, father of [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]] (the rightful heir to throne). Officially died at sea along with his wife Verona centuries ago, but circumstances highly suspicious. Seen in Bane Watcher shared vision on palace balcony with his father (High King) and young [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]], later fleeing on boat during storm while being pursued by unknown ship. Said "I'm sorry" to [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]] before she turned pursuing ship to stone with magic. True name unknown - records only refer to him as "second son."
