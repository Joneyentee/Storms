## Description

Halfling with crooked nose and missing right hand (lost cutting potatoes according to [[NPCs/Pippa Porridgepot\|Pippa Porridgepot]]). Husband or partner to [[NPCs/Pippa Porridgepot\|Pippa Porridgepot]], family member of the Porridgepot clan running [[Locations/Crossroads Inn\|Crossroads Inn]]. Disappeared 3 months ago searching for the "mythical Garlic Patch." Transformed into the [[NPCs/Moth Queen\|Moth Queen]] through unknown means (Bane influence suspected), defeated by [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] in [[Locations/Moth Queen Cavern\|Moth Queen Cavern]].
