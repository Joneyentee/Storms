## Description

Village elder and mayor of Frostglade. Old man with white beard, dressed in green, white, brown, and gold - the original Santa Claus colours rather than modern red. Kind and welcoming to newcomers.

**Realm:** [[Locations/Fizwidget's Winter Realm\|Fizwidget's Winter Realm]] (Session 33)  
**Canon Status:** Exists only in Fizwidget's pocket dimension  
**May Return:** If Fizwidget brings party back or NPC escapes to main world

---

## Session 33 Appearance

Pip and Palla introduced the party to Clausonick after the husky race. As village elder and mayor of Frostglade, he welcomed the newcomers alongside Jack Frost the Frost Baker. Oversees the cheerful, happy village inhabitants and their annual winter traditions.
