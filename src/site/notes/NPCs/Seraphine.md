## Description

Wandering druid healer who arrived at [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] after the mercenary attack to provide aid. Lives on the outskirts in a simple hut filled with apothecary supplies. Falsely accused of [[NPCs/Jon Doe\|Jon Doe]]'s murder by [[NPCs/Erland Greystone\|Erland Greystone]] due to being a newcomer, but cleared by [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] investigation. Blonde-haired human woman with kind demeanour. Possesses shapeshifting amulet (raven design) allowing transformation into raven or small bear.

**Recruited Session 31:** After being cleared of suspicion, [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] invited her to join party as dedicated healer. Initially declined (village needs her), but persuaded (roll 13) to join [[Locations/Stormhold Keep\|Stormhold Keep]] outfit. Set up with apothecary space at Keep, continues healing surrounding villages through health potion shipments.
