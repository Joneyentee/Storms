#### Description
Duke Vallandor Firebloom is the Lord of the entire northern territories of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]], owning [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]] and commanding vast military resources. One of the highest-ranking nobles beneath royalty, he wields significant political power. Competes in tournaments and is aligned with [[NPCs/Ser Alvar Flowers\|Ser Alvar Flowers]]'s conspiracy faction.

#### Key Details
- Owns all northern territories including [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]]
- Very high-ranking noble (just below royalty in hierarchy)
- Identified as member of [[NPCs/Ser Alvar Flowers\|Ser Alvar Flowers]]'s coup faction
- Tournament competitor - competed in Grand Joust
- Lost to [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] in Round 2 after leading 4-0 (hit Co'narr in head twice), then was demolished when Co'narr rolled two natural 20s
