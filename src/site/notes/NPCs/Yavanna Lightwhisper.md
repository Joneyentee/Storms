## Description

Twin sister of [[NPCs/Ser Alvar Flowers\|Ser Alvar Flowers]], bastard daughter of Gaelin (High King's first son, deceased) and [[NPCs/Elincia Flowers\|Elincia Flowers]] (the White Falcon Knight). Part of the Falcon faction conspiracy attempting to seize throne through her brother's claim and mother's leadership. **Granddaughter** of [[NPCs/High King Everglade\|High King Everglade]] through illegitimate line.

Previously encountered by [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] at casino where they had relations, with him completely unaware of her identity or connection to the conspiracy at the time. Current whereabouts unknown during civil war and palace siege.
