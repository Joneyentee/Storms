## Description

Ancient evil incarnate - pure corruption and chaos given form. Not a dragon, demon, or god, but something more fundamental. Has existed since before recorded history and has had many names across different races and times. Simply called "the Bane" by [[Organisations/Bane Watchers\|Bane Watchers]] as umbrella term. Corrupts everything it touches, feeds on chaos and destruction. Released when horn was blown (party's involvement). Primary world-ending threat.

## Key Details
- Nature: Evil itself, pure corruption incarnate
- Powers: Corrupts everything, feeds on chaos, overruled entire dwarven race
- History: Imprisoned by dragon gods after great war, one dragon god died in battle
- Current status: At large, location unknown
- Detection: Strong presence in [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]], seen in underground dwarven roads
- Has had many names throughout history

## Historical Impact
- Corrupted the dwarven race completely in ancient times
- Led to war between corrupted dwarves and elves
- Both races nearly wiped out (explains current rarity)
- Imprisoned by dragon gods' sacrifice
- Released when party blew the horn
