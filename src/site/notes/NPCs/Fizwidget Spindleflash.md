### Description

Gnome trickster with reality-warping powers. Appears in top hat, flashy posh suit with white ruffle collar, handlebar moustache, white hair, big beard, and red nose. Carries pocket watch that controls time. Alice in Wonderland White Rabbit aesthetic with theatrical flair. Creates magical pocket dimensions and alternate realities with snap of fingers. Enjoys chaos, defying order, and challenging mortals with games where failure has permanent consequences.

### Key Details

- **Reality Manipulation:** Created entire alternate reality from feast through toadstool realm lasting several hours
- **Carnival Master:** Commands magical carnival with thousands of gnome family members as spectators
- **Time Control:** Pocket watch allows him to freeze time, create temporal loops, and manipulate perception
- **Connected to [[NPCs/Barry\|Barry]]:** Likely avenging gnome shot in eye by [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] during early tournament melee outside [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]]

- **True Nature Unknown:** Possibly Fey creature, Archfey, trickster god, or exceptionally powerful gnome wizard
- **Current Location:** Unknown (disappeared after giving rewards)
