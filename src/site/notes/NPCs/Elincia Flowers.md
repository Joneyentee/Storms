**TRUE IDENTITY:** All three are the SAME PERSON

1. **Thalas** - The Black Knight (heavy black plate armour, flaming 2H sword)

**Session 26 Revelation:** Helmet melted away by acid damage during throne room battle, revealing face recognized by four party members. Identity confirmed as woman they'd previously met as "Lady Alysanne" - the White Falcon Knight who gave them evidence scroll. **All three identities are the same person playing multiple sides of civil war.**

## Description

Master manipulator playing both sides of Odessia's civil war. As **Lady Alysanne** (White Falcon Knight), led Falcon faction publicly. As **Elincia Flowers**, mother of bastard claimants [[NPCs/Ser Alvar Flowers\|Ser Alvar Flowers]] and Yavanna Lightwhisper. Wears imbued heavy black plate armour as Thalas, conceals identity behind helmet. Wields magical 2H sword capable of flame attacks. Escaped Session 26 via fire teleportation after identity exposed.
