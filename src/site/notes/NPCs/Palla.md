## Description

Small snow sprite child who welcomed party to Frostglade village alongside brother Pip. Cheerful and enthusiastic about inviting newcomers to participate in annual snowfall games.

**Realm:** [[Locations/Fizwidget's Winter Realm\|Fizwidget's Winter Realm]] (Session 33)  
**Canon Status:** Exists only in Fizwidget's pocket dimension  
**May Return:** If Fizwidget brings party back or NPC escapes to main world

## Session 33 Appearance

Palla and Pip (snow sprite siblings) greeted the party upon their arrival in Frostglade village after the husky sled race. They introduced the party to Clausonick (the village elder) and Jack Frost (the Frost Baker). The two children invited the party to participate in Frostglade's annual snowfall games, announcing that the newcomers would be "the stars of the show for the village."
