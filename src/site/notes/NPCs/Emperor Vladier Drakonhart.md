## Description

6'5" tall, middle-aged with dark-brown hair. Emperor of [[Locations/Immortal Draconic Empire\|Immortal Draconic Empire]]. Renowned for being war-like. Cheated in chariot race against party and holds grudge. Political enemy of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]] and the [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]]. Currently imprisoned but still dangerous.
