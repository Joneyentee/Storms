## Description

Young pompous knight. **Former** Lord of [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] and surrounding lands (stripped of title/lands, declared for Falcon faction). Retainer of [[NPCs/Ser Alvar Flowers\|Ser Alvar Flowers]]. Part of 'new breed' of lords (new money), not liked by villagers or old lords. Currently status as traitor after civil war erupted.

**Note on Name:** Also referenced as "Anfarlan" in some records.
