## Description

Veteran elf knight of many battles who commands respect. Knight under [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]], currently looking after [[Locations/Stormhold Keep\|Stormhold Keep]] in party's absence. Serves as military advisor who briefs party on regional issues including bandit activity, cathedral town rebellion, and missing persons. Senior military liaison between crown and party who advocates disciplined, preventative military action. Commanded opposing force in mock battle (Session 28) and soundly defeated party, providing tactical debrief on their failures (don't leave openings, avoid getting surrounded). Recruited 20 additional footmen bringing keep forces to 732 total. Presented [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] with four stewardship candidates for [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]], strongly recommending Ser Oldric the Stalwart.
