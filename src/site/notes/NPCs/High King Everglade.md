## Description

700+ year old elf king of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]], appearing old despite elf longevity. Over the top and gregarious personality, but power and majesty had been waning in old age. People were looking towards [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]] (his granddaughter) for future of kingdom before his death.

**Death:** Assassinated by [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] in throne room (Session 23), NOT from illness as previously believed. [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]] had ordered his death and [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] carried out the assassination.
