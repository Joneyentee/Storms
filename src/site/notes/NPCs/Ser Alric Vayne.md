### Description

Former second in command of the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]], imprisoned for approximately three weeks for siding with the Crown against High Commander [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]]'s rebellion. Gaunt and malnourished from his imprisonment, with visible marks of a beating, but sharp-eyed and mentally alert. Blunt, angry, and direct - introduces himself and his allegiances without preamble. Formerly handled much of the Order's recruitment administration, writing flyers for cities across the region. Despite his physical deterioration, organised the freed prisoners rapidly and decisively once released.
