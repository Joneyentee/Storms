## Description

Man who could transform into a wyvern. Killed by [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] during siege on [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]]. Killing blow delivered by [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]], making her most famous person in city. Kept detailed diary with darkest secrets. Had death certificates prepared for party members.
