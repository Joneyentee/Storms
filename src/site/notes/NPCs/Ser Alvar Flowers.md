## Description

Middle-aged but young-looking and calculated knight. Wears chromed armour with crimson plumed helmet. **Bastard son** of Gaelin (High King's first son, deceased) and [[NPCs/Elincia Flowers\|Elincia Flowers]] (the White Falcon Knight). **Granddaughter** of [[NPCs/High King Everglade\|High King Everglade]]. Co-leader of Falcon faction conspiracy attempting to seize throne of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]] through force. Commands retainers including [[NPCs/Ser Anfalen\|Ser Anfalen]].

Initially attempted to win Grand Tournament to gain influence over Kingdom. When tournament was suspended due to civil war, escalated to full military assault on palace with mother [[NPCs/Elincia Flowers\|Elincia Flowers]] leading Falcon forces.
