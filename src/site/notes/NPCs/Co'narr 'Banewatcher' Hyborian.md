## Description

7'8" tall Goliath barbarian and leader of the [[Organisations/Bane Watchers\|Bane Watchers]]. Led Bane Watcher initiation ritual for party, revealing extensive lore about [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]], dragon gods, and ancient history. Currently competing in tournament to force military alliance contract with kingdom. Has personally seen [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] in underground dwarven roads.

**Session 26 Addition:** Acquired permanent kitten companion that won't leave his side and hisses at everyone else. Demonstrated advanced Bane knowledge, warned [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] about corruption progression, urged party to visit Bane Watcher Citadel for training.

## Bane Knowledge Revealed (Session 26)

**Nature of Bane Watchers:**
- "Sad existence" - difficult path
- Can shorten lifespan
- More attuned to Bane = worse corruption becomes
- Corruption can "overflow and take over" the host
- Risk increases with deeper connection

**About The Bane:**
- Living entity
- "The earth itself"
- Origin unknown ("don't know where it came from")
- Manifests as green sprites in corrupted individuals
- Progressive corruption - starts subtle, becomes overwhelming

**Sprites as Manifestation:**
- Green fairy-like sprites indicate Bane presence
- Normal part of corruption progression
- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]]'s own sprite: Drunk mini-version with tankard
- Indicates level of corruption/attunement

**[[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]'s Assessment:**
- Tested party with Bane-infused drink
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] showed green sprite manifestation
- **Warning:** "You're further along than I thought"
- Urgent need for Citadel training
- Implication: Her corruption advancing dangerously fast

**Bane Watcher Citadel:**
- Located: Island in center of large lake (central kingdom)
- Island has mountain range
- Home of Bane Watcher organization
- Purpose: Train members to master abilities
- Leadership: Bane Watcher leader resides there
- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]]'s role: Wanderer/scout (not based at Citadel)

**Training Necessity:**
- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] cannot provide advanced training
- Citadel has resources and expertise he lacks
- Urged party to visit "quickly"
- Timing flexible but increasingly urgent for [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]

### Bane Test Results (Session 26)

**Test Method:** Passed tankard with Bane-infused drink to each party member

**Results:**
- [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]]: Tasted nothing (no effect, low corruption)
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]: Tasted nothing (no effect, low corruption)
- [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]: Tasted nothing (no effect, low corruption)

- **[[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]]:** Green sprite appeared (drunk mini-version with tankard on shoulder)

**Interpretation:**
- Three party members: Minimal Bane corruption currently
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]: **Significantly corrupted** - "further along than I thought"
- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]]: Also corrupted (expected as Bane Watcher leader)
