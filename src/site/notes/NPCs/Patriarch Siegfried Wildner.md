## Description

48 years old, tall with striking silver hair and blue eyes. Leader of the school of Evocation at the Mage's Tower in [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]]. Renowned as very ambitious person who pushes boundaries of fire, air, water, and thunder magics. Created his own spells. Ambitions reach outside of Mage's Tower, often seen around castle. Determined to leave a legacy that will change magic within this world.

**Current Status:** **Recovered with unexplained power increase** (Session 25) after appearing to sacrifice himself during Session 23 resurrection ritual. Made dramatic battlefield entrance riding elk, destroyed approaching siege wyvern with single devastating beam spell showing significantly enhanced magical power. Led reinforcement forces including [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] into enemy rear lines during palace siege. **Power source unknown** - what happened during unconscious state remains mystery.
