### Description

High Commander of the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]] in [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]]. Tall, jet-black hair, human male. Originally sent to the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]] as a young page, eventually rising to lead the Order. Has a short fuse, deep mistrust of outsiders, and - according to both [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] and [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] - is connected to or leading a secret cult. Over the past month has systematically turned the Order against the Crown, imprisoned or eliminated dissenters, shut the garrison gates, and reduced the Order's visible numbers. Described by [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] as "rot wearing the Order like a mask."

### Key Details

- Turned the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]] against the Crown before news of the High King's death reached [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]]
- Multiple Order members killed, gone missing, or imprisoned under his leadership; those remaining appear to be willing followers or complicit in the Order's new direction
- Accused by [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] of being part of a secret cult; [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] independently describes the Order's change as something he cannot fully explain
- The [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]] garrison banners were found defaced with a green cult symbol - the same symbol [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] identified on the garrison corridor wall (S37)
- The unfinished journal found on the guard room desk (S37) may be connected to Darius or a close subordinate
- Imprisoned [[NPCs/Clara\|Clara]] for burning food - disproportionate punishment suggesting personal or cultist motivation
- A figure in brown and gold armour bearing the royal arms of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]] in wrong colours (blue and silver instead of green and gold) was observed watching a torture session in the garrison - identity unconfirmed but potentially Darius Kain or a superior
- Confirmed [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] operative (S38): was acting under the Ash Protocol, tasked with silencing outsiders interfering with the western fissure; wielded a flaming mace with ability to summon a fire entity ("Cinder to me") and call down a column of fire
