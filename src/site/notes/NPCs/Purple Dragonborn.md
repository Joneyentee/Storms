### Description

A massive purple-scaled dragonborn standing approximately twelve feet tall, armoured in heavy plate. Previously known only as a petrified figure glimpsed in [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s Bane Watcher initiation vision (S18), calling out Elik'r's name. Encountered physically for the first time in S38 when the [[Ember Vein Envoy\|Ember Vein Envoy]] animated him from an ancient stone statue in the [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] underground complex beneath [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]]. Whether he is a hero, a villain, or something else entirely remains unknown.

### Key Details

- Height: 12 feet tall; purple scales (non-standard, homebrew)
- Carries a longsword with a dark iron blade shot through with ember-red veins of light; the blade radiates heat and the sound of slow breathing can sometimes be heard from within the metal
- **[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s family crest** is present on a clasp on his armour — confirmed by Elik'r on sight (S38); no other party member recognised it
- Fought in an ancient war; petrified at an unknown point in history by an unknown woman's spell
- In Elik'r's Bane Watcher initiation vision (S18), screamed directly at him by name: _"RELEASE ME, RELEASE ME, ELIK'R STORMSWALLOW!"_
- The seraph communicated the word _"Release"_ to [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] via an ember fissure (S38) — believed to refer to this figure
- Animated by the [[Ember Vein Envoy\|Ember Vein Envoy]] with the spoken word _"Arise"_ at S38 session end; boss encounter not yet resolved
- Fought against dwarves and other races in an ancient war — allegiance and cause unknown
