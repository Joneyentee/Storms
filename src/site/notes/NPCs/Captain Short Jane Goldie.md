### Description

Female dwarf who seized command of an enemy vessel during the party's escape from the volcanic island (Session 1). Originally part of the crew left behind when the party's ship ran aground, she and other survivors took over one of the pursuing dwarf vessels and agreed to evacuate the party. Sailed south, then changed course east before landing at [[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]]. Last seen when party disembarked at [[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]] port.

### Key Details

- Original Role: Crew member on party's first ship
- Crisis Action: Took captain's role after original captain killed/captured during sea battle
- Vessel: Captured enemy ship (from pursuing dwarf fleet)
- Notable Leadership: Organized surviving crew to seize enemy vessel
- Escape Route: Sailed south, encountered more ships, changed course east to [[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]]
- Cargo: Two horses in hold (party took one, forgot the other - ship sailed before they returned)
- Current Status: Unknown - ship sailed from [[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]] while party was in town

**Sea Battle & Shipwreck:**

- Party's ship attacked at sea, most crew killed/captured/abandoned ship
- Party ran ship aground on volcanic island (couldn't steer properly)
- Left surviving crew behind while exploring mountain

**Ship Seizure:**

- While party was in mountain, surviving crew (including Short Jane) seized one of enemy vessels
- Short Jane took command as acting captain
- Agreed to take party aboard for escape

**Escape Journey:**

- Party escaped from mountain as it began erupting
- Boarded captured ship with Short Jane as captain
- Two horses were in the hold
- Sailed south initially
- Encountered more ships, ToooAh captured briefly but talked way out
- Changed course east to avoid pursuit
- Landed at [[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]]

**Departure:**

- Party took one horse when disembarking
- Forgot about second horse
- Ship (with second horse) sailed before party returned to docks
