## Description

Tall, very attractive human male bard with theatrical, lordly personality. Wears maroon shirt with extensive gold inlays and cowboy-style hat with long feather. Carries multiple instruments including lute, flute (holstered on leg like pistol), violin/fiddle. Speech is very posh with hint of campness - theatrical, mischievous, roguish. Claims to be "sensation around entire kingdom" with exploits known throughout [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]]. Can pull items from magical hat. Uses enchantment magic and powerful bardic abilities including portal creation.
