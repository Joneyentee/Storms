## Description

24-year-old ruler of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]], soon to be coronated as Queen (coronation delayed by civil war). **Granddaughter** of the late [[NPCs/High King Everglade\|High King Everglade]]. Long, flowing lilac hair from the matriarchal line of royal family. Young and slim with well-toned body. The epitome of elven grace and symbol of hope amongst the people. Increasingly reliant on the [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] to stabilise Odessia.

**Tournament Identity:** Revealed as the **Eagle Knight** during jousting tournament (Session 21) - competed anonymously with eagle heraldry. Her absence from royal feast (Session 19) was because she was the Eagle Knight competitor.
