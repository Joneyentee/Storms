### Description

The head servant of [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]]'s [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]] mansion, responsible for managing the household and preparing accommodations for the party during the tournament. He survived the coordinated attack on the mansion by jumping from a window, sustaining severe injuries including multiple lacerations, cuts, and embedded glass shards. Currently unconscious but stabilized, he is the only surviving witness to the mansion attack.
