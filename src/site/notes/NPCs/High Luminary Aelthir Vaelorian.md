### Description

482-year-old elf and head of the Cathedral of Gentle Redemption in [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]], holding the title High Luminary and Voice of the Dawn Choir. Silver-gold hair worn in immaculate braids adorned with orbs. Clear, resonant voice that carries perfectly throughout the cathedral. Presides over the town with the authority of a lord holding court as much as a religious leader.
