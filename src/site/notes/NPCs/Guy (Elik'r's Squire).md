### Description

Guy was [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s primary squire, a loyal and capable human warrior who proved his combat prowess in his final moments. He served faithfully during the [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]] tournament, assisting with armour and standing ready to protect his master. In the town square ambush, he demonstrated quick reflexes and lethal efficiency before being fatally stabbed from behind while engaging multiple attackers.

### Key Details

- Primary squire to [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]], more experienced than [[NPCs/Ser Garet of the Wildlands\|Ser Garet of the Wildlands]]
- Killed an assassin by "lamping him down" and stomping on his head/neck
- Stabbed in the back during [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]] town square ambush (Session 22)
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] attempted to heal him with Cure Wounds potion but he died from his wounds
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] wanted to stay with his body but [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] knocked him unconscious and carried him away
