## IMPORTANT: Identity Revelation

**"Kendara Elley Aloison" was never a real person.** This was a cover identity created by [[NPCs/Professor Gabriel Emms\|Professor Gabriel Emms]] for the magical doll that would later be transformed into [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]]. The entire "missing daughter" story was a protective ruse.

## The Cover Story (What Party Was Told)

[[NPCs/Professor Gabriel Emms\|Professor Gabriel Emms]] approached party in [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] after the raid, claiming:

- His "daughter" Kendara was missing
- Age 4 years old
- Distinctive lilac/purple hair
- Last entrusted to friend [[NPCs/Ryvenien Berg\|Ryvenien Berg]]
- Disappeared during or after [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] raid

## The Truth

**"Kendara" was the magical doll:**

- Age 4 and lilac hair matched what the doll would become when transformed
- No villagers in [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] recognized the name or description
- [[NPCs/Ryvenien Berg\|Ryvenien Berg]] did have something entrusted to him - but it was the doll, not a child
- Professor Emms' last words: "They took the doll" (not "They took my daughter")
- The doll was found by [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] on smuggler boat (before Professor's quest began)

## Why the Deception?

**Protective Cover:**

- Easier to ask adventurers to find "missing child" than "magical doll"
- Avoided revealing doll's true significance
- Protected [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]]'s identity (royal heir)
- Prevented conspirators from knowing what they were really seeking

**The Description Matched:**

- 4 years old = doll's apparent age when transformed
- Lilac hair = royal bloodline trait (matches [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]]'s actual appearance)
- Tall for age = doll's actual size
- Professor knew exactly what the doll would become
