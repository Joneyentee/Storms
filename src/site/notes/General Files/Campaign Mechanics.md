## Campaign-Specific Mechanics

### Bane Watcher Initiation Ritual

**Purpose:** Grant Bane Sense ability to new members

**The Ritual:**

1. Drink dragon blood mixture (from dead dragon god)
2. Make Constitution saving throw
3. **Failure:** Take damage (5+ observed), extended unconsciousness, possible death
4. **Success:** Still unconscious 4-20 hours
5. During unconsciousness: shared prophetic vision
6. Wake with permanent Bane Sense ability

**Party Results (Session 18):**

|Character|Roll|Result|Unconscious Duration|
|---|---|---|---|
|[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]|Natural 20|Survived easily|20 hours|
|[[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]|20|Survived easily|~6 hours|
|[[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]]|15|Survived with difficulty|7 hours|
|[[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]|13|Struggled but survived|~6 hours|
|[[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]|Failed|Took 5 damage|4 hours|

**Bane Sense Ability:**

- Permanent passive ability (always active, cannot turn off)
- Allows sensing [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]'s presence
- No range limit
- **Current Stage:** Overwhelming sensory overload (like hearing all prayers at once)
- **Development:** Improves with training and experience
- No mechanical bonus yet (story/RP ability)

### Shared Prophetic Visions

**Trigger:** Dragon blood ritual side effect

**Nature:**

- All participants experience identical dream
- Don't initially realize it's shared
- Shows past events, prophecies, hidden truths
- Cannot control what is seen
- Duration varies per participant (4-20 hours)

**Purpose:**

- Bane Watcher training and initiation
- Reveals hidden threats and connections
- Provides plot hooks and lore

- Constantly hears own voice: "It's your fault"
- Acquired Session 23 (ethereal plane trauma)

**Trauma-Triggered Temporary Petrification ([[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]):**

- Can become temporarily petrified (frozen, unaware) when triggered
- Trigger: Traumatic flashback + Bane influence + green glow on spells
- Acquired Session 25 - Self-resolving with successful Wisdom save
- Natural 20 save resulted in rapid recovery (Session 25)
- May recur under similar conditions
- See [[General Files/Homebrew\|Homebrew]] for full details

**Green Glow Enhancement/Corruption ([[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]):**

- Green spectral glow appears on offensive spells
- First appeared Session 24
- Effects unpredictable (enhancement or petrification)
- Progressive - appearing more frequently
- **Reflection Pattern Confirmed (Session 34):**
    - Wisdom saving throws required during spellcasting with green glow
    - First two failed saves: resisted successfully
    - Third failed save: full spell damage reflected back to caster
    - Session 34: Failed 3rd Wisdom save (rolled 5), 32 damage from Agonising Scorcher reflected, knocked unconscious
- **Escalation (Session 36):** Corruption now manifesting through innate draconic ability (Dragon's Breath), not only arcane spellcasting. Dragon's Breath produced green Bane flame - knocked [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] unconscious. Suggests corruption spreading beyond spellcasting to Elvva's fundamental draconic nature.
- See [[General Files/Homebrew\|Homebrew]] for full details

**Psychological Curse - Combat Trigger ([[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]):**

- Voice of guilt curse triggers during combat when causing friendly fire
- Session 30: Hit Elvva with Fire Bolt (Natural 1), heard "Ah, it's my fault again!"
- Deals 3 psychic damage consistently when triggered
- Specifically triggers when harming Elvva (person he failed to save Session 22)
- No known cure, active combat threat

**Bane Physical Manifestation ([[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]):**

- [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] flowed visibly from Tessell's palm into the stonework of the [[Locations/Silver Lantern Garrison\|Silver Lantern Garrison]] corridor wall (Session 37), activating a hidden green cult symbol and opening a sealed passage
- [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] retracted back into his hand as the last party member entered, sealing the passage behind them
- Tessell was the only party member able to sense the symbol; [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] and [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] (both Bane Watchers) perceived nothing
- Mechanism unknown - why Tessell specifically, and whether this can recur, is unestablished
- Distinct from Bane Sense (passive detection) - this was an active, physical outflow
- **Note:** The DM's audio confirms "green Bane" not "green vein" - the manifestation is explicitly The Bane

**Ember Fissure Contact (Session 38 — NEW MECHANIC):**

- Touching an ember fissure in the [[Locations/Ember Vein Underground Complex\|Ember Vein Underground Complex]] triggers direct psychic contact from the seraph
- **Mechanic:** White dice roll determines whether the character receives any vision at all; if contact occurs, a Wisdom saving throw follows
- **On failed Wisdom save:** Character takes psychic damage (16 observed on Natural 1; 14 observed on dirty 20 insufficient)
- **Vision content:** Rapid, fragmented psychic imagery — the majority too fast to process; a few distinct images resolve clearly per individual
- **Individual messages:** The seraph appears to address each person differently — [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] received _"Release"_; [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] received _"Kill them all"_
- **External observation:** Nothing visibly happens to the person touching the fissure
- Mechanic may recur anywhere ember fissures are present

## Resurrection Mechanics

### Necromantic Resurrection (Session 23)

**Requirements:**

- High-level casters (11+ observed)
- Multiple support mages
- Safe location for bodies during ritual
- Constitution/Wisdom saves during journey
- Combat capability in ethereal plane

**Risks:**

- Not guaranteed to succeed
- Bane influence stronger in ethereal realm
- Psychological trauma (witness subject's traumatic history)
- Officiating mage may sacrifice themselves
- Bane can plant dormant commands in minds

**Consequences Observed:**

- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]: Successfully resurrected, must take Warlock level 2, witnessed 200+ year trauma
- [[NPCs/Patriarch Siegfried Wildner\|Patriarch Siegfried Wildner]]: Unconscious with no vital signs (recovered Session 25 with power increase)
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]: Cursed with guilt voice
- Three party members: Dormant Bane commands planted
- All party: Gained understanding of resurrection's true cost

## House Rules & Rulings

### Death Saves

- Standard D&D rules apply
- DC 10 at start of turn when at 0 HP
- 3 successes = stabilized
- 3 failures = dead
- Natural 20 = regain 1 HP
- Natural 1 = 2 failures

### Resurrection Availability

- Necromantic resurrection possible but extremely dangerous
- Standard clerical resurrection (when available) generally accepted
- Party has witnessed consequences firsthand

### Milestone Leveling

- No XP tracking
- DM determines level advancement based on story progression
- Last advancement: End of Session 23 (Level 5 → 6)

## Quick Reference

### Action Economy

- **Action:** Attack, Cast Spell, Dash, Dodge, Help, Hide, etc.
- **Bonus Action:** Certain spells, class features
- **Reaction:** Opportunity Attack, Shield spell, etc.
- **Movement:** Based on speed (typically 30 ft)

### Advantage/Disadvantage

- Roll 2d20, take higher (advantage) or lower (disadvantage)

### Critical Hits

- Natural 20 on attack roll
- Double all damage dice (not modifiers)
