## Odonia's Warg Ability

**Type:** Homebrew (replaces druid multiclass) **Acquired:** Session 30 (green Minotaur ooze transformation during dream vision)

### Mechanics (Revealed Session 31)

**Tattoo:** Shifting tattoo on chest (wolf/eagle/serpent morphs constantly)

**Warging:**

- Project consciousness into creatures within 30ft
- Body falls unconscious while warging
- No psychic damage when warging ends (creature dies or cancelled)

**Control Mechanic (Session 31):**

- Roll grey die when warging
- **Even result** = Full control over creature
- **Odd result** = Creature's hostile instincts take over, may attack allies

**Spirit-Bonded Creature Discovery (Session 36):**

- Attempting to Warg into a creature that is spiritually bonded to another being (e.g., the orc rider's Frost Wolf) does not grant control
- Instead: caster is pulled into a **green spirit realm** and meets the bonded creature's spiritual owner
- The spiritual owner can attack directly in this realm - [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] took **12 psychic damage** from the orc rider's spirit
- Warg attempt fails; caster is ejected from the spirit realm
- Implies Warg ability interacts with spiritual/Bane-adjacent forces, not just animal minds

**First Combat Test (Session 31):**

- Warging into Earthbound Dire Wolf
- Rolled 3 (odd number)
- Lost control, wolf attacked [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] for 15 damage instead of intended target

**Origin:**

- Crushed under debris during Khorrak battle in dream vision
- Khorrak's corrupted green ooze seeped under rock to unconscious body
- Body whisped away in green smoke upon transformation
- Transformation triggered by: prolonged exposure + vulnerable state + corrupted entity death nearby
