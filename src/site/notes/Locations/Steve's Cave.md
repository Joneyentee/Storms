## Description

Small outcrop cave on opposite ledge from [[Locations/Dream Temple\|Dream Temple]] in shared dream/vision realm. Contains boulder with face called "Steve" by [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]], though unclear if Steve is real entity or [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] talking to himself. Cave has fluid portal door leading back to temple. Writing in unknown language on cave wall. Cave appears empty when [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] not present, but "Steve" reportedly makes later solo appearances.
