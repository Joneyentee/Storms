## Description

Partially repaired after previous damage. Stone provided by [[Locations/Ulvenora\|Ulvenora]] mining town. Houses **752 troops** (Session 28 - 20 additional footmen recruited) and serves as base of operations for [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]], **Marshals of the North**. Walls under construction with scaffolding, stonemasons actively working. Main keep wing renovated (no roof holes), still unfurnished with basic cots.

## History

**Original Status:** Double-walled castle design, abandoned for 700+ years, original name lost to time

**Early Campaign Occupation (Session 3):**

- Occupied by bandit mercenaries led by Logrin the Dwarf
- Black Knight ([[NPCs/Elincia Flowers\|Elincia Flowers]] as Thalas) present with magic portal
- Party tracked bandits here after [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] raid

**Castle Clearing Battle (Session 3):**

- Party attacked with [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]]'s forces
- **Memorable Moment:** [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] fell over during initial charge before reaching enemy
- **Legendary Nat 1:** [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] rolled Natural 1, failed to hit any enemies entire combat, only struck [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]] with friendly fire
- Black Knight escaped through portal
- Bandits cleared, castle secured

**The Portal Discovery:**

- Dwarven writing over door: "Say Friend and enter"
- Trick: Had to say "friend" in Elvish ("Mellon") not Dwarvish
- Portal only opened in darkness
- [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]]'s magical tea caused 3-day thunderstorm, darkened sky, enabled portal opening
- [[NPCs/Elincia Flowers\|Elincia Flowers]] (as Lady Alysanne/White Falcon) asked party to investigate with [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]]
- Portal led to underground dungeon where [[NPCs/Professor Gabriel Emms\|Professor Gabriel Emms]] and [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]] transformation ritual occurred

**White Falcon Quest Rewards (Session 3):**

- **2,000 gold coins reward**
- **Scroll 1:** Permission to operate in the land (operate like knights)
- **Scroll 2:** King assigned castle and surrounding lands to [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] as their base
- **Mission:** Protect western kingdom (capital, port town, Sylobelle, mining town)

**Castle Renaming:**

- Original name lost to history
- Renamed: **Stormhold Keep** by [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]]

**Initial Garrison (Session 3):**

- [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]] commanded initial forces
- 25 troops healed and rested after battle
- Grew to 45 footmen after [[Locations/Ulvenora\|Ulvenora]] recruitment + warhorse

**Castle Layout (Session 3):**

- **SW - Inner court:** Troops camp
- **Centre:** Pavilions/tents, only foundations remaining (no buildings)
- **E - Keep:** Barely structurally sound but best building, mainly ruined, some rooms, leaking roof
- **NE:** Former mercenary storage, open to air, 3 weeks' rations found
- **NW - Old stables:** Makeshift corral, three horses (Ser Silvar's, party's, Tessell's warhorse)
- **Inner walls:** Fairly solid
- **Gatehouse:** Battered and crumbled but structurally sound sides
- **Outer walls:** Crumbled and holey
- **Entrances:** North (main) and South (river for trade/supplies)
- **Drawbridges:** Both down
- **Moat:** All around castle, mostly filled in

**Main Tasks Identified (Early Campaign):**

- Repair keep
- Build covered storage
- Reinforce walls (repair holes)
- Hunt for food/supplies

Granted to [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] by [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]] along with army. Previously damaged (circumstances unclear). Currently being rebuilt with stone from [[Locations/Ulvenora\|Ulvenora]].

**Session 26:** **Official deed of ownership ratified** by [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]]. Party commissioned as **Marshals of the North** with formal military ranks. Received 636+ troop reinforcements (200 archers, 32 knights+squires, 420 footmen). War chest authorized for northern campaign expenses. Now serves as headquarters for northern rally mission.
