### Description

An ancient underground complex of unknown age situated beneath [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]], accessible via a blood-magic sealed door in the [[Locations/Silver Lantern Garrison - Hidden Passage\|Silver Lantern Garrison - Hidden Passage]]. Structurally old but in active use by the [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] cult. The complex is threaded throughout with glowing green fissures — the ember veins themselves — which pulse with slow rhythmic light, drain the life of the uninitiated who pass through them, and transmit direct psychic contact from the seraph to those attuned to ancient powers. A near-identical site is known to exist beneath [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]], confirmed by [[NPCs/Calen Thorne\|Calen Thorne]].

---

### Geography

Entirely subterranean; no natural light. Illuminated throughout by green Bane-fire and the pulsing ember fissures in the walls and floors. Carved from stone of considerable age — the construction predates the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]] and likely the town of [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]] itself. The complex sits below the garrison's lowest floor, deeper than the building's foundations would suggest possible. Depth and full extent unknown.

### Key Features

- **Three-Staircase Junction** _(entry point, part of [[Locations/Silver Lantern Garrison - Hidden Passage\|Silver Lantern Garrison - Hidden Passage]])_ — Central hub connecting the hidden passage staircase (from garrison), a left staircase ascending toward the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]], a right staircase of unknown destination, and the blood-magic sealed archway leading deeper into the complex
- **Sealed Stone Archway** — First gateway into the complex proper; no visible lock or mechanism; opens only via blood pressed to the stone; exhales audibly as it opens; marked with a brownish stain consistent with prior blood use
- **Necrotic Corridor** — Narrow stone passage connecting the sealed archway to the draconic archway; hairline fissures of green Bane-fire pulse along floor and walls; drains the life of those without Bane attunement (Constitution save required); party members with Bane attunement emitted a brief protective green glow and passed unharmed
- **Draconic Archway** — Heavy stone arch with no door; carved with approximately twenty distinct dragon reliefs (wyrms, wyverns, four-legged and two-legged varieties, varied breath weapons); reacts to blood — the most weathered carving, a wingless fire-breathing dragon, is the activation point; Elik'r's blood parted the archway; each other person passing through triggered the carved dragons to animate and breathe green fire (Dexterity save or take fire and psychic damage); [[NPCs/Calen Thorne\|Calen Thorne]] passed through with a brief unexplained green glow
- **Communal Area** — Small mess room with rough-hewn tables and remnants of cups and provisions; clearly in regular use but not permanently occupied; four wooden side doors and one larger ornate door leading deeper
- **Barracks Room** — Carved bunk alcoves, simple storage trunks, black and red robes; partially burned letter found (Draconic, signed "Serif, Third Ember")
- **Study Room** — Shelved alcoves with books, tomes, and codexes; six writing desks; an open book left mid-read containing [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] doctrine on the seraph's entombment and the nature of the ember veins
- **Torture Chamber** — Divided room; one cell contained a dead man (tortured over several days, journal recovered); second cell empty with female clothing (nun's habit) left behind — likely [[NPCs/Clara\|Clara]]'s; torture implements scattered on a table opposite
- **Leader's Office** — More ornate than other rooms; single desk, orderly condition; sealed [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] letter recovered from desk drawer (Draconic, authorising the Ash Protocol, signed "The Inner Ember")
- **Ritual Chamber** — Large chamber at the complex's deepest known point; stone table used for ritual sacrifice; four pedestals surrounding an ancient dragonborn statue; back wall concealed by a permanent illusion (dropped by the [[NPCs/Ember Vein Envoy - Unnamed\|Ember Vein Envoy - Unnamed]] S38); site of combat with [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] and the session-end confrontation

### Population

No permanent residents. Used periodically by [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] operatives — the communal area and side rooms show signs of regular use but not continuous habitation. The writing across all recovered documents is from different hands, suggesting rotating personnel rather than a fixed garrison.

### History

Age unknown. The [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] cult's own doctrine describes the complex as connected to a network of ember fissures — subterranean "veins" of ancient fire that maintain the slumber of a vast entity called the seraph, entombed beneath an old caldera. Three convergence points have been mapped beneath the Caldera by the cult (per recovered journal, Entry 61). The complex predates all current institutions in [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]] and connects to at least one equivalent site beneath [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]]. Blood magic is integral to the complex's design — both entry points require it.
