## Description

Hidden headquarters of the [[Organisations/Bane Watchers\|Bane Watchers]] organization, located on a mountainous island in the centre of a large lake in central [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]]. Ancient citadel dedicated to training members in managing Bane corruption and mastering abilities that [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] cannot teach. Home to the Bane Watcher leader and organization's accumulated knowledge about [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]. Urgent destination for [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] whose corruption is advancing faster than expected.
