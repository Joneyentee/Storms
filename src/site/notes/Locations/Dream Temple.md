## Description

Vast supernatural temple accessible only through shared dreams/visions, not physical travel. Multi-chambered realm beginning at mountainside ledge over endless chasm, progressing through Hall of Mirrors, Gardens Chamber, Hall of Statues, The Depths labyrinth, and Central Chamber. Each chamber contains puzzle or trial requiring problem-solving to advance. **Party successfully escaped Session 30** after defeating [[NPCs/Khorrak the Horned Dread\|Khorrak the Horned Dread]] and solving dragon's forgiveness riddle in Central Chamber. Connection to [[Organisations/Bane Watchers\|Bane Watchers]] abilities or other supernatural forces unclear - purpose appears to be testing truth, courage, memory, passage, and forgiveness.

### Central Chamber (Chamber 5 - Final Test)

**Layout:**

- Large chamber with big dragon statue in centre
- Four pedestals arranged around dragon statue
- Each pedestal has dragon talon/claw designed to grip items
- Dragon statue initially has closed wings
- 10ft ceiling
- Inscriptions in dwarfish on pedestals

**Dragon Statue:**

- Large ornate dragon at centre
- Wings closed initially, open when gems placed
- Neck articulates to look at each person simultaneously
- Stone face becomes animated
- Eyes glow blue when shedding tear

**The Four Pedestals:**

- Each has dragon talon/claw
- Grip gems when placed
- Surrounding dragon in square formation
- Purpose: Hold dragon chamber gems (keys)

**Puzzle Mechanics:**

- Party arrived with 6 of 8 gems ([[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] lost 2 when transformed)
- Placed 4 gems in 4 talons (6 available - chose which 4)
- Dragon statue animated when gems placed:
    - Wings opened
    - Neck moved, looked at each person simultaneously
    - Spoke in eerie voice (each heard own language)

**The Riddle:**

- Dragon asked: "What is it that cannot be obtained through mere strength but requires the shedding of something more precious?"
- Warning: "I'm weary, answer only once, or be doomed forever"
- Extended claw palm-upward (suggesting physical offering)
- Party answered: "Forgiveness"
- Dragon responded: "Show me" (required demonstration, not just words)

**Solution:**

- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] demonstrated genuine forgiveness to [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]
- Said: "I forgive you for killing me that one time. It's not your fault anymore. I no longer blame you for my death"
- Referred to Session 22 when [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] failed to save her during Belendar ambush
- Dragon judged forgiveness authentic
- Stone face glowed blue (faint at first, becoming brighter)
- Eye glowed blue progressively brighter
- Shed single tear from one eye
- Tear landed in [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]'s hand, formed into gem (**Dragon's Tear**)
- Walls started feeling like they were disappearing
- Vision ended - party woke in physical bodies

**Exit Mechanism:**

- No door or portal visible
- Reality itself dissolved when puzzle solved
- Party members transported back to physical bodies
- Woke in separate locations: [[Locations/Stormhold Keep\|Stormhold Keep]] (four members), [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]] ([[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]])

**Items Lost/Gained:**

- All 8 dragon chamber gems disappeared upon waking
- **Exception:** Dragon's Tear remained in [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]'s possession
- Only item from entire vision that persisted to reality
- Appears alive inside, light moves and shifts
