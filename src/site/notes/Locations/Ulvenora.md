## History

Long-standing mining town serving [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]]'s construction needs. Traditional stonemason community.

**Early Campaign (Session 2-3):**

- **Stone Negotiations:** Party arranged supply deal for [[Locations/Stormhold Keep\|Stormhold Keep]] reconstruction
    
    - **Stone:** 20,000 gc (1,000 gc per week for ongoing work)
    - **10 Stonemasons:** 5,000 gc
    - **Down Payment:** 5,000 gc paid (mainly from [[Party Members/Ser Pas Rata\|Ser Pas Rata]]'s tournament gambling winnings)
    - **Cost Reduction:** 7,500 gc discount negotiated in exchange for solving orc problem
- **Orc Quest:** Beast men with tusks and brown skin wearing animal skins/leather armour came down from mountains
    
    - Unknown language, shouting same phrase repeatedly
    - Translated from Orcish: "Kill them all for the holy Garrash"
    - Party went into mountains, found orc family
    - **Legendary Mistake:** At least two party members spoke Orcish fluently, could have attempted communication, never thought to try talking
    - Killed orcs, returned quest complete
    - Secured manpower and quarried stone supply
- **Recruitment Success:** After completing quest, [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]] recruited additional troops, bringing total to 45 footmen plus warhorse for party
