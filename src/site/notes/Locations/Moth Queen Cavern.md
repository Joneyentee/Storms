## Description

Cavern near [[Locations/Crossroads Inn\|Crossroads Inn]] known as "The Cavern of the Fluttering Menace" with "Glory to the Moth Queen" etched above entrance. Former lair of [[NPCs/Moth Queen\|Moth Queen]] (transformed [[NPCs/Oswick Porridgepot\|Oswick Porridgepot]]) responsible for potato field raids. Explored and cleared Session 34 by [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]]. Narrow single-file passages filled with itchy dust, flesh-like cocoons, pit trap, and main cavern containing mounds of half-eaten vegetables and rotting animal carcasses.

## Geography

- Initial passage: Narrow (single-file only), covered in itchy dust (walls, ceiling, floor)

- Passages: Winding, not wide enough for two people side-by-side
- Main cavern: Large chamber with mounds of half-eaten potatoes/vegetables
- Flesh-like cocoons: 1ft tall, wobbling throughout cavern

**Explored:** Session 34 (cleared)

## Key Features

- **Inscription:** "Glory to the Moth Queen" above entrance
- **Itchy Dust:** Covers walls, ceiling, floor throughout passages (Constitution save DC required)
- **Narrow Passages:** Single-file only, winding paths
- **Pit Trap:** Right-hand hole where floor disappears (Dexterity save to avoid falling)
- **Flesh-like Cocoons:** 1ft tall, wobbling, scattered throughout
- **Main Cavern:** Large chamber with:
    - Mounds of half-eaten root vegetables and potatoes
    - Rotting animal carcasses
    - Fluttering and whispering sounds
    - 1ft wide moths flying past
- **Oil Lamp:** Found in potato pile with "Oswick Porridgepot" engraved (kept by [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]])
