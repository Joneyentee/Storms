## Description

Identical small rooms for each competitor (separate instances) used for Tournament of Wits and Wisdom. No physical connection between competitor rooms (complete isolation). Five sequential rooms with different elemental themes. Possibly magical pocket dimension or hidden castle space controlled by [[NPCs/Eldrin Oakenhart\|Eldrin Oakenhart]].

- Eleven walls per room that light up magically
- Five sequential rooms per instance
- Identical layout for each competitor
- Complete isolation between instances

## Key Features

### Room Progression

**Room 1 - Cold/Blue Door:**
- Blue illumination
- Cold damage environment (5 points per wrong answer)
- Room fills with ice on wrong answers
- Temperature drops, causes shivering

**Room 2 - Fire/Red Door:**
- Red/orange illumination
- Fire damage environment (5 points per wrong answer)
- Intense heat
- Flames appear on wrong answers

**Room 3 - Lightning/Yellow-Static:**
- Yellow/static illumination
- Lightning damage environment (5 points per wrong answer)
- Electrical sparks in air
- Lightning bolts zap competitors on wrong answers

**Room 4 - Earth/Brown-Orange:**
- Brown/orange illumination
- Earth/crushing damage environment (5 points per wrong answer)
- Room constricts, squeezes competitors
- Crushing sensation on wrong answers

**Room 5 - Final Challenge:**
- Variable damage type
- Unknown illumination pattern
- Final test

## Mechanics

**Challenge Format:**
- Riddles appear on doors
- Correct answer opens next door (for that competitor only)
- Wrong answers inflict 5 elemental damage
- Individual progression (no waiting for others)
- Upon completion, teleportation back to feast hall

**Access & Control:**
- [[NPCs/Eldrin Oakenhart\|Eldrin Oakenhart]] has control (voice projection, monitoring)
- [[NPCs/Ser Alvar Flowers\|Ser Alvar Flowers]] can bypass via portals
- Unknown if others have access
- Can be monitored from outside (how else track completion?)

**Entry/Exit:**
- Drugged competitors at feast
- Awakened in challenge rooms
- Teleported back to feast upon completion
