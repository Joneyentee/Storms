### Description

The central public square of [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]], featuring a large ornamental fountain, statues, and open cobblestone plaza. This busy marketplace and gathering space serves as a hub for five major roads and is typically filled with street vendors, market stalls, and citizens. During festivals like the Grand Tournament, it becomes even more crowded with entertainment and celebration. As of Session 22, it is now the site of a major violent incident that shattered the festival atmosphere.

### Geography

Located in the heart of [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]], at the junction of multiple major streets. The square is large enough to accommodate crowds of hundreds, with a prominent central fountain serving as the focal point. Market stalls and vendor carts typically line the perimeter. Cobblestone paving throughout. Streets radiate outward in multiple directions.

### Key Features

- **Central Fountain:** Large ornamental fountain, used by [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]] to execute captured assassin by drowning (Session 22)
