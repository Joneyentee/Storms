## Description

The planet on which the D&D campaign takes place. Ruled by an all-powerful god, previously known as "Mr Delero". Home to the continent of [[Locations/Eros\|Eros]] and possibly other undiscovered continents.

## Geography

Currently only [[Locations/Eros\|Eros]] continent is known. Whether other continents exist is unknown to the party.

## Key Features

- Contains the continent of [[Locations/Eros\|Eros]]
- Ruled by divine being (Mr Delero)
- Standard fantasy world with magic

## Population

Multiple races including humans, elves, dwarves, dragonborn, halflings, gnomes, aarakocra, and others. Population spread across known continent [[Locations/Eros\|Eros]].

## Leadership

- **Divine Ruler:** Mr Delero (all-powerful god)
- **Mortal Rulers:** Various kings, queens, and emperors across kingdoms

## History

Created through word and song according to bardic tradition. Long history including the First Age when dwarves and elves were the original races, followed by [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]'s corruption and the dragon gods' war.

## Current Situation

[[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] has been released and threatens the world. Multiple kingdoms exist across [[Locations/Eros\|Eros]] with varying allegiances and conflicts.
