## Description

Old dwarven highway network beneath the surface of [[Locations/Eros\|Eros]]. Ancient infrastructure from when dwarves ruled the world before [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]'s corruption. Extensive tunnel system connecting ancient dwarven cities. Now fallen into disrepair, dangerous, with active [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] presence.
