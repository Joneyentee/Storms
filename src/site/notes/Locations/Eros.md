## Description

Currently the only known continent on planet [[Locations/Lorellia\|Lorellia]]. Home to three main kingdoms from north to south: [[Locations/Nolunia\|Nolunia]], [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]], and [[Locations/Immortal Draconic Empire\|Immortal Draconic Empire]].

## Geography

**Northern Region:** [[Locations/Nolunia\|Nolunia]] - colder climate (presumed), humans and centaurs, independent city-states

**Central Region:** [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]] - temperate forests, multiple rivers, agricultural land, mountain ranges in northwest, balanced and hospitable climate

**Southern Region:** [[Locations/Immortal Draconic Empire\|Immortal Draconic Empire]] - climate unknown, hostile territory

**Natural Features:**
- Central lake with [[Organisations/Bane Watchers\|Bane Watchers]] citadel on island
- Deep forests (especially around [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]])
- River systems throughout (including River Silverdue)
- Mountains in northwest
- Western coastline
- Badlands to the east

## Key Features

- Three major kingdoms with different governments and cultures
- Ancient dwarven underground highway network
- Mix of races across continent
- Center of magical learning in [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]]

## Population

Mixed races throughout, with regional variations:
- North: Mainly humans and centaurs
- Center: Mixed races (elves, humans, halflings, dragonborn, etc.)
- South: Human-dominated (other races killed or enslaved)

## Leadership

- **North:** No centralized leadership ([[Locations/Nolunia\|Nolunia]])
- **Center:** [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]] ([[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]])
- **South:** [[NPCs/Emperor Vladier Drakonhart\|Emperor Vladier Drakonhart]] ([[Locations/Immortal Draconic Empire\|Immortal Draconic Empire]])

## History

**Ancient History (First Age):**
- Dwarves and elves were first races
- [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] corrupted dwarves, leading to near-extinction of both races
- Dragon gods imprisoned [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]
- Dwarves built extensive underground highway network

**Current Era:**
- Three kingdoms established
- [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] recently released
- Political tensions between kingdoms

## Current Situation

- [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] at large (world-ending threat)
- [[Locations/Immortal Draconic Empire\|Immortal Draconic Empire]] hostile to [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]]
- [[Locations/Nolunia\|Nolunia]] independent confederation
- Party based in [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]] working to secure territories
