## Description

Northern territory of [[Locations/Eros\|Eros]]. Less a kingdom, more collection of independent towns and cities forming free confederation. Mainly humans and centaurs. Free and competitive culture with individual achievement valued.

## Geography

**Location:**
- Northern [[Locations/Eros\|Eros]]
- North of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]]

**Terrain:**
- Likely colder climate (northern region)
- Various terrain types
- Multiple independent settlements

## Key Features

- Free confederation structure
- Independent city-states
- No centralized capital
- Individual achievement culture
- Competitive society

## Population

Mainly humans and centaurs. Free peoples with independent governance. Mixed population across independent settlements.

## Leadership

Long tradition of independence and freedom. Confederation of free peoples rather than centralized kingdom. Values individual achievement and self-governance.
