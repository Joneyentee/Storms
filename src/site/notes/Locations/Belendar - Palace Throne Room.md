## Description

The grand throne room of the Odessia royal palace, a magnificent chamber designed to project power and majesty. High vaulted ceilings with ornate pillars line the sides, while the throne sits elevated at the far end on a dais. **This chamber is forever marked as the site where High King Everglade was assassinated** (Session 23) **and where Thalas's true identity as Elincia Flowers was exposed** (Session 26). The room is large enough to accommodate hundreds for court proceedings and has served as the site for formal ceremonies including knighting rituals and noble elevations (Session 26).
