### Description

The fortified garrison headquarters of the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]] in [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]], situated within the town and adjacent to the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]]. Once an open, publicly accessible building in keeping with the Order's founding principles of protection and service, the garrison gates are now permanently shut under [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]]'s command. The building has the atmosphere of an occupied institution - familiar in form, deeply wrong in function.

### Geography

Stone-built fortification within [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]]. A back entrance via a waterway provides access to the garrison's water supply and leads directly into the basement level. The basement sits below the main garrison floor, which is accessed via staircases.

### Key Features

- **Back Entrance** - Waterway access point to the basement; guarded by approximately two knights or squires; the entry point used by the [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] during infiltration (S37)
- **Basement Cell Blocks** - Two separate cell blocks discovered (S37): the first holding [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] and approximately eight imprisoned Crown-loyal Silver Lanterns; the second holding an elderly couple, a one-armed man, and a cell presumed to have held [[NPCs/Clara\|Clara]] (found empty, bloodstained, with a lock of golden hair)
- **Torture Chamber** - Locked room with a solid door and barred window; man observed chained to wall and being beaten by three guards (S37); a figure in brown and gold armour bearing the royal arms of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]] in wrong colours (blue and silver) was observed watching from a desk - identity unconfirmed
- **Guard/Break Room** - Weapons rack, cauldron of food, writing desk; Silver Lantern banners found defaced with a green cult symbol; an unfinished journal was recovered from the desk (S37)
- **Green Cult Symbol** - Etched into the right-hand corridor wall at basement level; glows green, visible only to [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]; activated by [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] flowing from his palm, opening a hidden passage to a lower level (S37)
- **Hidden Passage** - See [[Locations/Silver Lantern Garrison - Hidden Passage\|Silver Lantern Garrison - Hidden Passage]]

### Population

Silver Lantern knights and squires loyal to [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]]. Crown-loyal members have been killed, imprisoned, or have disappeared. Total active garrison guard count estimated at approximately 10 on rota (per [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]], S37).

### Leadership

- **High Commander:** [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] - turned the Order against the Crown; accused of cult affiliation
- **Former Second in Command:** [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] - imprisoned, subsequently freed (S37)

### History

The [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]] originated as a group of monks who went abroad on crusade and returned committed to protecting and healing people rather than conquest - functioning similarly to crusader military orders such as the Hospitallers. The garrison served as their operational base and was historically open to the public. Within the past month, [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] turned the Order against the Crown, shut the gates, and began imprisoning or eliminating dissenting members.

### Current Situation

Under [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]]'s control. Multiple guards killed during the [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]]' infiltration (S37). Freed prisoners and the party have descended into the hidden passage below. The garrison above was alerted by [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s Thunderous Smite - reinforcements were converging on the basement at session end. The man in the torture chamber remains locked inside by [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]'s permanent lockpicks.
