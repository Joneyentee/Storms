## Description

Three-story tavern at junction of 5 roads, also known as Burrow and Barrel Inn. Run by the halfling Porridgepot family. Distinctive mushroom-shaped roof. Famous for food, drink, entertainment (bareknuckle boxing), and magical experimental pastries. Strategic location at major crossroads.
