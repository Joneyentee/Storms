### Description

Former noble residence of [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]] in [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]], used to house the [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] during the Grand Tournament. The mansion featured multiple floors, servants' quarters, and garden areas typical of elvish noble architecture. As of Session 22, the building has been completely destroyed by fire in a coordinated attack, with the roof collapsed and multiple servants killed. Only the major-domo survived by jumping from a window into the back garden.
