## Description

The chivalrous, elvish kingdom in central [[Locations/Eros\|Eros]]. Peaceful kingdom that welcomes all races, known as center of learning and magic. Located between northern [[Locations/Nolunia\|Nolunia]] and southern [[Locations/Immortal Draconic Empire\|Immortal Draconic Empire]]. Mixed-race population with elven cultural dominance.

## Geography

**Terrain:**
- Temperate forests (especially around [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]])
- Farmland ([[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] region)
- Mountains/foothills (northwest, [[Locations/Ulvenora\|Ulvenora]] region)
- Western coastline ([[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]])
- River systems throughout

**Key Locations:**
- **Capital:** [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]]
- **Northwest:** [[Locations/Stormhold Keep\|Stormhold Keep]], [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]], [[Locations/Ulvenora\|Ulvenora]]
- **West:** [[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]] (port)
- **Central:** [[Locations/Crossroads Inn\|Crossroads Inn]], [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]]
- **East:** [[Locations/Eastern Citadel\|Eastern Citadel]] (borders Badlands)

## Key Features

- Mixed-race society with equality under law
- "Elvish City of all Nations" ([[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]])
- Center of magical learning (Mages' Tower)
- Chivalrous culture with knightly orders
- Grand Tournament held in [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]]

## Population

Mixed races: Elves (dominant), humans, halflings, dragonborn, dwarves (rare), gnomes, and others. All races welcome and treated equally.

## History

Long-standing elvish kingdom that has maintained peace and welcomed all races. Recently experienced:
- Siege on [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]] (repelled by [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]])
- Death of [[NPCs/High King Everglade\|High King Everglade]] (secret: ordered by daughter)
- Rising political tensions (Eagle vs Falcon factions)
- Release of [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]
