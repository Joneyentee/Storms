### Description

A seemingly ordinary tavern in [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]] that conceals a secret underground safehouse used by the [[Organisations/Bane Watchers\|Bane Watchers]] organization. The establishment operates as a functioning public tavern while maintaining a hidden basement accessible via trapdoor behind the bar. The barman operates the entrance and provides refuge for [[Organisations/Bane Watchers\|Bane Watchers]] and their allies during emergencies. Features both underground basement level and upper floor with balcony overlooking main tavern area. Its existence and location appear to be known only to trusted members of the organization.

### Geography

Located somewhere within [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]]'s city limits, specific district unidentified. Two-story tavern structure with underground basement. Main floor contains bar area with hidden trapdoor, large distillery barrels (~8ft tall, immovable/fixed to floor), and tables. Upper level accessed by rear staircase, features wooden balcony with half-cover railings providing elevated defensive position. Back area includes pantry/storage room with food supplies. Multiple entry points: front door (main thoroughfare), back door (toward alley/storage).
