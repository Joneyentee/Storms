## Description

Academic and magical district of [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]] featuring the famous Mages' Tower. Center of magical learning and research. Houses magical shops and academic institutions.

## Geography

**Location:**
- Within [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]]
- District centered on Mages' Tower

**Features:**
- Mages' Tower (dominant landmark)
- Magical shops
- Academic buildings
- Research facilities

## Key Features

### Mages' Tower
- Towering circular building
- Each floor = different school of magic
- Each school = different color
- Multicolored skyscraper visible across city
- Houses all eight schools of magic
- [[NPCs/Patriarch Siegfried Wildner\|Patriarch Siegfried Wildner]] leads Evocation school
- [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]] currently training here

### Other Features
- Magical shops and suppliers
- Academic institutions
- Libraries and research facilities
- Student housing
