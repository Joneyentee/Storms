## Description

Farming town in northwest [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]], 120 miles NNE of [[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]]. **Duchy of Lady [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]** (elevated Session 26). Agricultural community that has experienced mysterious murders and supernatural events. Currently experiencing active disappearances and murders (Session 28) - fisherman found dead, bodies missing, presumed murders. **Former lord Anfarlan stripped of title** for declaring for [[NPCs/Ser Alvar Flowers\|Ser Alvar Flowers]] during civil war.
