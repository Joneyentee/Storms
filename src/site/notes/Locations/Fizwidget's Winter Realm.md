## Description

Magical winter-themed pocket dimension created by [[NPCs/Fizwidget Spindleflash\|Fizwidget Spindleflash]] as a testing ground for elaborate challenges and games. Features perpetual winter conditions with snow-covered landscapes, frozen lakes, mountain slopes, and the cheerful village of Frostglade. Age regression effect forced all inhabitants to teenage years (16-17) with equipment replaced by fur coats.

---

## Geography

**Terrain:** Snowy fields, frozen lake (half-mile wide), steep mountain slopes with multiple descent paths, glacier formations with ice pillars  
**Climate:** Perpetual winter, blizzards, extreme cold  
**Magical Properties:** Age regression field, ability suppression (flight, fire magic), equipment transmutation

**Notable Landmarks:**
- **Frozen Lake** - Half-mile wide with unstable ice, creature beneath (fin sighted)
- **Mountain Slope** - Three descent paths (cavern left, snow/woods centre, rocky/misty right)
- **Glacier Pass** - Ice pillars with falling icicles
- **Frostglade Village** - Festive winter settlement with cheerful inhabitants
- **Village Centre Arena** - Snowball tournament battleground with six large ice pillars

---

## Key Features

- **Age Regression Field** - All visitors reverted to teenage years (16-17) while maintaining memories
- **Equipment Transmutation** - All gear removed and replaced with fur coats upon entry
- **Ability Suppression** - Flight and fire abilities magically neutralized
- **Blizzard Barrier** - Pursuing wall of sharp ice and blizzard driving inhabitants forward
- **Magical Pastries** - Jack Frost's creations provide random buffs/debuffs for 1 hour
- **Reality Control** - Fizwidget has complete control over environment, physics, and inhabitants
