## Description

Former wolf den near [[Locations/Sylobelle\|Sylobelle]] that became site of earth elemental corruption. Contained broken earth shrine with Orcish "EARTH" inscription and ritualistic bone arrangement in wolf shape. After [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] repaired shrine (Session 31), cavern became lair of Earthbound Dire Wolf before collapsing. Site of mysterious earth magic rituals with unknown origins.
