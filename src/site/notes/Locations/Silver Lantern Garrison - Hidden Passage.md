### Description

A hidden passage discovered beneath the basement of the [[Locations/Silver Lantern Garrison\|Silver Lantern Garrison]] in [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]], accessed via a green cult symbol etched into the corridor wall. The passage descends below what should be the building's lowest level and terminates in a three-staircase junction chamber — a central hub that connects to the [[Locations/Ember Vein Underground Complex\|Ember Vein Underground Complex]] via a blood-magic sealed archway, to the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]] via a left-hand staircase, and to an unknown destination via a right-hand staircase. The green-flamed lanterns that ignited automatically on entry burn with pale Bane-fire — visible to all, but felt as a presence only by those with Bane attunement.

### Geography

Accessed from the right-hand wall of the basement corridor of the [[Locations/Silver Lantern Garrison\|Silver Lantern Garrison]]. Descends via staircase to the three-staircase junction — a small square chamber with pale green Bane-fire lanterns. Three routes lead away: the sealed entry staircase (above), a left-hand staircase ascending toward the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]] (choral singing audible from above), and a right-hand staircase of unknown destination, surmised by [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] to exit somewhere outside the town. A blood-magic sealed stone archway in the junction wall leads to the [[Locations/Ember Vein Underground Complex\|Ember Vein Underground Complex]].

### Key Features

- **Entry Point** — A green symbol etched into the garrison corridor wall; visible only to [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]; activated exclusively by [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] flowing from his palm
- **Self-Sealing Door** — The wall sealed shut behind the last person to enter, with [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] retracting into Tessell's hand; cannot be opened from inside by known means
- **Green-Flamed Lanterns** — Ignited automatically along the staircase the moment the passage sealed; burn with pale Bane-fire; visible to all but perceptible as a presence only to those with Bane attunement
- **Three-Staircase Junction** — Small square chamber at the base of the entry staircase; three routes plus the sealed archway; currently held by [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] and freed Silver Lanterns as a rearguard position
- **Left-Hand Staircase** — Ascends toward the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]]; choral singing audible from above; provides a known exit route
- **Right-Hand Staircase** — Destination unknown; surmised to exit outside the town
- **Blood-Magic Sealed Archway** — Stone door with swirling carved patterns and a green fissure; no visible lock or mechanism; opens only when blood is pressed to the stone; connects the junction to the [[Locations/Ember Vein Underground Complex\|Ember Vein Underground Complex]]

### Population

The three-staircase junction is currently held by [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] and the freed Crown-loyal Silver Lanterns, positioned as a rearguard. No permanent inhabitants.

### Leadership

Unknown.

### History

Unknown age. The passage and junction predate the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]]'s occupation of the garrison and connect to an [[Locations/Ember Vein Underground Complex\|Ember Vein Underground Complex]] of considerable antiquity. The cult symbol at the entry point matches those found defaced on garrison banners, confirming [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] involvement. The blood-magic construction is consistent with the complex below — [[NPCs/Calen Thorne\|Calen Thorne]] confirmed his mentor found a near-identical site in the chasms beneath [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]], suggesting this is part of a network rather than an isolated structure.

### Current Situation

[[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] and the freed Silver Lanterns hold the three-staircase junction as a rearguard. The [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] and [[NPCs/Calen Thorne\|Calen Thorne]] have moved through the blood-magic archway into the [[Locations/Ember Vein Underground Complex\|Ember Vein Underground Complex]]. The left-hand staircase to the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]] remains a known exit route.
