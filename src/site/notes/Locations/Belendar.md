## Description

Capital of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]]. "Elvish City of all Nations" - center of learning and magic. Nestled in deep forest with intertwining rivers. Multi-tiered urban center with beautiful stonework throughout. Dominated by the multicolored Mages' Tower visible across the city.

## Geography

**Setting:**
- Deep forest location
- Multiple intertwining rivers flow through city
- Natural defensive position
- Multi-tiered architecture

**Climate:**
- Temperate (central [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]])

## Key Features

### Mages' Tower
- Towering, circular building housing all schools of magic
- Each floor represents different school of magic
- Each school has different color
- Result: Multicolored skyscraper visible across city
- Currently houses [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]] (training with [[NPCs/Patriarch Siegfried Wildner\|Patriarch Siegfried Wildner]])

### City Gates
Four main gates, each assigned a letter in the coded correspondence found during warehouse investigation:
- **Silven Embrace (S)** - Gate location unknown
- **Glimmering Vale (A)** - Eastern gate
- **Silverdew Crossing (B)** - Northern gate beside River Silverdue (**targeted by Balthazar Thornwood/Wyvern during siege**)
  - Strong, high wall and gate
  - Reinforced wooden drawbridge
  - Iron gate
  - Beautiful stone-worked bridge with silver inlays
- **Eldergrove Arch (H)** - Main gate (north)

### Royal District
- **Castle** - Royal seat of [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]]
- Throne room where royal feast held
- Palace balcony (seen in Bane Watcher vision with [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]])

- **Underground Tavern Hideout** - Secret [[Organisations/Bane Watchers\|Bane Watchers]] safehouse, accessed via trapdoor, large cellar, friend of [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]]

## History

Ancient elvish city, long-standing capital of [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]]. Recently experienced:

**Grand Tournament (Sessions ~9-14):**
- Major multi-event tournament hosted at [[Locations/Belendar - Victory Square\|Belendar - Victory Square]]
- Events: Jousting, dancing, chariot racing, melee, wits competitions
- [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] won dancing competition and chariot race
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] placed second in dancing
- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] surprising third in dancing
- **[[NPCs/Emperor Vladier Drakonhart\|Emperor Vladier Drakonhart]]** cheated in chariot race (grappling hook and rope) - caught, imprisoned
- Royal feast attended by all contestants - **everyone drugged and fell unconscious simultaneously** (including Crown Princess)
- Tournament of Wits and Wisdom puzzle dungeon (Sessions 19)

**Warehouse Investigation & Elvva Joins (Session ~15):**
- Crown Princess summoned party (Eldrin, Patriarch, and [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] present - Shannon's first session)
- Suspicious deliveries and hooded figures at docks warehouses
- Party investigated, uncovered four-faction war evidence and gate attack plan

**Riverside Shack (Session ~15):**
- Missing people quest near riverbank (500gc reward, local guard stamp)
- Shack on sticks in water with boardwalk, books and test tubes inside
- **Human skeleton found under floorboards** - blue/white heraldic crest with Eagle symbol
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] stung and poisoned by cactus-type plant during combat with hostile flora/fauna
- Journals found but indecipherable

**Siege of Belendar (Sessions 16-17):**
- City attacked simultaneously at all four gates
- Party defended Silverdew Crossing (coded 'B' correspondence had identified Balthazar's target)
- [[NPCs/Ser Balthazar Thornwood\|Ser Balthazar Thornwood]] transformed into wyvern at Silverdew Crossing
- [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] delivered killing blow - repelled attack, became most famous person in city

**Post-Siege:**
- Siege by [[NPCs/Ser Balthazar Thornwood\|Ser Balthazar Thornwood]] (wyvern form) - repelled
- [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] delivered killing blow (now most famous person in city)
- Grand Tournament subsequently disrupted by civil war events
