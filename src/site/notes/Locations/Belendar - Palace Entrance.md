### Description

Main entrance to the royal palace, featuring grand doors leading to central halls and throne room approach. During Session 25, this entrance became one of three coordinated assault points led by a Black Knight wielding a flaming greatsword. Heavy fighting concentrated here as Falcon Faction forces attempted to breach the main palace doorway while royal guards defended the entrance.

### Geography

Grand entrance with large reinforced doors, positioned at end of palace bridge approach. Stone archway and defensive architecture designed to funnel attackers. Entrance leads to main palace halls connecting to throne room and other key areas.

### Key Features

- **Grand Entrance Doors** - Large reinforced doors (main palace access)
- **Defensive Archway** - Stone architecture creates natural chokepoint
- **Connection to Throne Room** - Main hall leads to central throne room
- **Tactical Chokepoint** - Entrance design favors defenders
