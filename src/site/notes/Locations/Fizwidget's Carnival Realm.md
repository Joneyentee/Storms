**Type:** Magical Pocket Dimension **Region:** Extra-dimensional space (accessed via Belendar) **Status:** Exists outside normal reality, access portal disappeared **Tags:** #location/magical #location/dungeon #location/mysterious

### Description

Magical pocket dimension created and controlled by Fizwidget Spindleflash, manifesting as a massive carnival realm with impossible physics and reality-warping properties. Main feature is enormous arena with tiered seating accommodating thousands of gnome spectators, surrounded by various challenge areas each with lethal stakes. Atmosphere shifts between whimsical carnival entertainment and deadly seriousness, reflecting Fizwidget's trickster nature and desire for theatrical chaos.

### Geography

**Physical Layout:**

- **Entry Room:** Empty chamber with central table, door at far end, miniaturization effect (party shrunk to tiny size until drinking growth potion)
- **Main Carnival Area:** Open space with colourful tents, strange creatures wandering, carnival music playing
- **Central Arena:** Massive amphitheatre with thousands of seats arranged in tiers, straw-lined performance floor
- **Challenge Zones:** Distinct areas for each trial (strongman platform, coconut shy range, unstable bridges, mirror maze pavilion, duck pond, knife-throwing platform)

**Environmental Properties:**

- Time manipulation (Fizwidget can freeze time at will)
- Reality warping (size changes, transformations, instant teleportation)
- Physics defiance (chocolate rivers, unstable floating bridges, impossible mirror reflections)
- Personalized perception (doors appear different to each viewer)

### Key Features

**Challenge Areas:**

- **Strongman Bell** - Hammer and bell mechanism for strength testing
- **Coconut Shy** - Three coconuts, four throwing attempts
- **Unstable Bridges** - Three sections of moving platforms over chocolate river
- **Mirror Maze** - Glass labyrinth requiring Intelligence to navigate
- **Hook a Duck Pond** - Participants transformed into ducks, gnomes with scythe-sized hooks
- **Main Arena** - Central performance space with thousands of gnome spectators
- **Knife-Throwing Wheel** - Spinning target wheel for accuracy trials

**Access Points:**

- **Toadstool Portal** (Session 27) - Wooden door in giant toadstool trunk, grown from magical bean in Belendar garden, disappeared after use
- **Unknown Other Entrances** - Fizwidget can create portals anywhere (demonstrated clicking fingers for teleportation)

### Challenge Mechanics (Session 27)

**Stakes System:**

- Each participant holds "token" representing life/safety
- Failure results in permanent loss (body parts, loved ones, abilities, death)
- Success preserves token
- Fizwidget shows illusions of potential losses before each challenge

**Observed Challenges:**

1. Strongman (Strength-based) - Ring bell with hammer
2. Coconut Shy (Accuracy-based) - Knock coconuts with thrown balls
3. Unstable Bridges (Acrobatics-based) - Cross moving platforms
4. Mirror Maze (Intelligence-based) - Navigate glass labyrinth
5. Hook a Duck (Athletics-based) - Swim as duck avoiding hooks
6. Arena Performance (Group skill checks) - Entertain gnome audience
7. Knife Throwing (Accuracy + chance) - Hit targets on spinning wheel

**Victory Conditions:**

- Individual: Succeed at challenge to keep token
- Group: Earn majority vote from gnome audience (706/1000 minimum)
- Fizwidget honours deals but chooses interpretations
