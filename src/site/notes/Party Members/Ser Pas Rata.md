## Campaign Significance

**THE BANE RELEASER:** Ser Pas Rata's decision to blow the horn (Session 1) is THE defining moment that created the entire campaign crisis. After [[NPCs/Joneyentee the Unknown\|Joneyentee the Unknown]]'s failed climb attempt, Pas Rata successfully retrieved the horn from the statue and blew it, causing:

- Entire mountain/island to shake violently and erupt as volcano
- Massive dragon ([[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]) to emerge and fly away
- Ancient evil released into the world
- All subsequent corruption, civil war, and suffering stems from this moment

**The Irony:** Joneyentee's overthinking (climbing instead of using Mage Hand) led to Pas Rata's impulsive action (blowing mysterious horn without investigation), combining to create world-ending consequences. The campaign's central crisis began with party's Session 1 choices.

**Legacy:** Though Pas Rata died roughly one month after leaving the party, his action in Session 1 continues to drive every major plot point. The Bane he released corrupts people, fuels civil war, threatens the kingdom, and haunts party members with visions and commands.
