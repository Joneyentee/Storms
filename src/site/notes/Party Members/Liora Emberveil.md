**Origin:** Grew up on the edge of a quiet human town, always fascinated by strange magic and the secrets of the arcane. When she was a teenager, a fleeting encounter with a mysterious fiend granted her powers she neither sought nor fully understood.

**Current Life:** Travels the world as a scholar and adventurer

**Personality:**

- Fascinated by strange magic and arcane secrets
- Scholar at heart
- Initially nervous/uncertain under extreme pressure (panic attack on frozen lake)
- Quick thinker and adaptive (Misty Step escape, branch somersault recovery)
- Protective of innocents

**Motivations:**

- Seeks knowledge to control her warlock gifts
- Uncovers ancient mysteries
- Protects those who cannot defend themselves
- Whispers of fiendish patron follow her every step

**Faith/Oath:** Pact with unknown fiendish patron (granted through fleeting encounter, not formal ritual)
