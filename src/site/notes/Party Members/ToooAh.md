## Physical Description

Aarakocra (bird-like humanoid) monk. Can fly 50ft per round. Parrot or eagle-like features with feathered body, long narrow legs with sharp talons.

## Background

Formerly part of gang with [[NPCs/Logrin the Dwarf\|Logrin the Dwarf]]. Brews magical teas with random d100 effects. Known for terminal velocity experiments and flying into trees while hunting. Adopted [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]] (animated doll brought to life).

**Military Rank:** Chariot General (Session 26 - appointed by [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]])
