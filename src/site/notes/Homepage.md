## The Story So Far

It began with a mistake. In the depths of a volcanic island, a party of strangers found an ancient underground temple. A carved statue held a horn. While [[NPCs/Joneyentee the Unknown\|Joneyentee the Unknown]] agonised over the best way to retrieve it, [[Party Members/Ser Pas Rata\|Ser Pas Rata]] simply climbed up and blew it. The mountain shook. The island erupted. A massive dragon - [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] - rose from within and flew free into the world. The party fled on a captured ship, sailed east, and never truly left that moment behind.

In the months that followed, the party - who would come to call themselves the **[[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]]** - found their way to [[Locations/Canathalas\|Canathalas]], competed in tournaments, cleared bandits from a crumbling castle that would become their home ([[Locations/Stormhold Keep\|Stormhold Keep]]), unravelled a mystery about a magical doll that became a child ([[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]]), and eventually made their way to the capital city of [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]]. There they competed in the Grand Tournament, investigated a conspiracy, fought off a four-gate siege, and killed a wyvern. They also drank dragon's blood, collapsed, and woke up as Bane Watchers - bound forever to the ancient evil their friend had accidentally unleashed.

The civil war that followed changed everything. The High King was assassinated. [[NPCs/Elincia Flowers\|Elincia Flowers]] - secretly the Black Knight Thalas, the tournament's White Falcon, and the architect of the conspiracy - escaped. The kingdom split 50/50 between the Eagle faction (loyal to [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]]) and the Falcon faction (loyal to [[NPCs/Ser Alvar Flowers\|Ser Alvar Flowers]]). The party emerged from the chaos as the **Marshals of the North**, granted an army, a castle, and a mission: rally the northern territories for the Princess before the kingdom tears itself apart.

They are currently sealed inside a hidden passage beneath the [[Locations/Silver Lantern Garrison\|Silver Lantern Garrison]] in [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]], having just broken one of their own out of a jail cell.
