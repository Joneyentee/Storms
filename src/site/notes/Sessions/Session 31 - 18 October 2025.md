### Combat: Earthbound Dire Wolf

**Initial Encounter:**

- Party exited the cavern
- Faced an **Earthbound Dire Wolf**
- The wolf appeared to be infused with earth magic

**Combat Highlights:**

**Opening Attacks:**

- Party readied actions when wolf burrowed underground
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]], [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]], [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]], and [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] detected wolf's emergence
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]: Chromatic Orb (Level 2) - 18 damage
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]: Firebolt - only 2 damage (one hit, one miss)
- [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]]: Dart at disadvantage - 6 damage
- Wolf attempted to grab [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] with earthen hand (failed, Strength save 18)

**Wolf's Attack:**

- Targeted [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]
- Howled and shot earthen rock spike from mouth
- **26 piercing damage** to [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]
- Spike embedded through her leg, reducing movement speed by half

**[[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]'s Turn:**

- Wolf entered his Warding Flare emanation (15ft radius)
- Wolf made DC 15 saving throw (succeeded, half damage)
- Took 4 radiant damage
- Wolf **bloodied** and yelped in pain

**Second Phase - Earth's Blessing:**

- When first bloodied, wolf became covered in molten earthen light
- Similar to the bleeding from the altar/shrine
- Wolf appeared infused with earth's power
- Gained resistance to damage while in defensive stance

**[[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]'s Warg Attempt:**

- Informed [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] of her warging ability
- Attempted to warg into the dire wolf
- Rolled 3 (odd number) on grey die - **PARTIAL CONTROL FAILURE**
- Successfully warged into wolf but retained the wolf's hostile instincts
- Party witnessed green glow emerge from [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]'s unconscious body
- Green hand grabbed wolf's snout, eyes turned bright green
- [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]'s perspective shifted to see through wolf's eyes
- Compelled to attack the party

**Warging Consequence:**

- [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] forced to use wolf's multi-attack
- Fired two **Earth Spikes** at [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]
- First attack (19) - hit for 15 piercing damage
- Second attack (12) - missed (AC 18)
- Wolf/[[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] attempted to burrow away

**Final Kill:**

- [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] took attack of opportunity as wolf burrowed
- Attack roll: 19 (hit)
- Damage: 6 bludgeoning
- **Described kill:** Grabbed wolf by tail and slammed it against the wall
- Wolf gave its last whimper
- Green wisp emerged from dying wolf
- Wolf's earthen green and brown colours faded to standard grey wolf colouring
- Lost all earth magic upon death

**Mysterious Observer:**

- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] spotted a figure on a distant mountain ridge
- **Huge wolf** with a **humanoid rider** on its back
- They had been watching the battle from afar
- Turned and disappeared from view when noticed
