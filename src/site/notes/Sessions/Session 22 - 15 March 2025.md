## Key Events

### Shared Vision of The Bane

- All [[Organisations/Bane Watchers\|Bane Watchers]] (party + [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]]) experienced simultaneous psychic vision after the joust
- Vision showed greenish haze, flashing images of combat, clanging weapons
- Voice commanded: "**Thalas, fetch the vessel**" and "**Thalas, kill the king**"
- All members took 14 points psychic damage (failed Wisdom saves below 15)
- [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] received mysterious note from unknown source delivered by royal squire

### Investigation Split

- Party split to investigate the vision's meaning:
    - [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] and [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] went to meet [[NPCs/Elincia Flowers\|Elincia Flowers]]
    - [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] and [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] went to [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]]
    - [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] stayed at pavilion

### Crown Princess Meeting ([[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] & [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]])

- Discussed the vision and threat to the king
- Princess doesn't know who [[NPCs/Thalas\|Thalas]] is, but believes Thalas is **a woman, not a man**
- Princess suspects three individuals as potential conspirators:
    1. [[NPCs/Elincia Flowers\|Elincia Flowers]] (known adversary)
    2. [[NPCs/Emperor Vladier Drakonhart\|Emperor Vladier Drakonhart]] (Dragon Emperor)
    3. [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] (surprising inclusion)
- Princess revealed mercenaries bearing **orange and blue colours** entering the city (Alvar's conspiracy colours)
- Princess cryptically stated [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] is "performing a task on my behalf" but refused to elaborate
- Princess immediately ordered 10 guards posted around the king, plus 5 specifically for the bird (ToooAh)
- [[NPCs/Eldrin Oakenhart\|Eldrin Oakenhart]] appeared when summoned

### White Falcon Meeting ([[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] & [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]])

- Met [[NPCs/Elincia Flowers\|Elincia Flowers]] in town square at 6:00 PM
- Accompanied by mysterious cloaked man with ornate gold cane, expensive purple clothes with gold embroidery
- Man addressed White Falcon as "**My Queen**" before leaving
- Man disappeared mysteriously into crowd (not invisibility spell - different magic)
- White Falcon gave [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] a scroll with evidence pointing to [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]] as [[NPCs/Thalas\|Thalas]]
- White Falcon warned of imminent danger
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] disguised herself as [[NPCs/Alarin Moonwhisper\|Alarin Moonwhisper]] (Tessell's squire) for protection

### Ambush in Town Square

- Multiple assassins attacked [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] and [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] in crowded square
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s squire (unnamed) killed one attacker by "lamping him down" and stamping on his neck
- **Guy (Elik'r's main squire) was stabbed in the back and mortally wounded**
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] killed attackers with Sorcerous Burst (one headless) and fire breath
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] strangled and burned attacker alive with fire breath
- [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]] and [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]] arrived as reinforcements
- **[[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] was killed** - stabbed multiple times while unconscious, failed death saves
- **Guy died from his wounds** despite [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]'s attempt to heal him with Cure Wounds potion
- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] executed combat combo: hammer strike on [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s shield creating shockwave that killed surrounding enemies
- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] knocked [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] unconscious and carried him to tavern hideout

### Ser Silvar's Mansion Burned

- [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] and [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] discovered [[NPCs/Ser Silvar Glinnan\|Ser Silvar Glinnan]]'s mansion on fire
- Major-domo found in back garden, critically wounded with lacerations and glass embedded (jumped from window)
- [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] stabilized him with Cure Wounds
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]'s squire caught up, relayed news of town square ambush
- Charred servant remains found inside mansion
- [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] and [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] brought wounded major-domo to tavern hideout

### Underground Hideout

- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] led survivors to secret tavern with trapdoor entrance
- Barman expected them and opened hidden basement
- Party regrouped in darkness underground
- Session ended with trapdoor closing above them
