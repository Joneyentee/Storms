### Encounter with Iron Finch Company

**Meeting on the Road:**

- Party caught up to a slower-moving covered cart with riders
- Guards waved in greeting - all human, wearing leather armour and weapons
- Driver: Bald-headed, heavily moustached individual in heavier armour than the guards
- Identified as **[[NPCs/Captain Mara Veylan\|Captain Mara Veylan]]** of the **[[Organisations/Iron Finch Company\|Iron Finch Company]]**
- Approximately 8 mercenaries in the back of the cart

**Conversation:**

- [[NPCs/Captain Mara Veylan\|Captain Mara Veylan]] asked [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] what kind of bird she is - mentioned birds are considered lucky
- This broke the ice and they agreed to travel together as far as [[Locations/Crossroads Inn\|Crossroads Inn]]
- Iron Finch Company hired by the Dukedom of [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]] as mercenaries
- Showed party a scroll from [[NPCs/Duke Alaric Thorne\|Duke Alaric Thorne]] of [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]] calling mercenaries to arms
- No information on scroll regarding payment or terms

**Strategic Information Gathered:**

**About [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]]:**

- Largest duchy in the north
- Critical ally for future battles in the civil war
- Human noble family rules as a dukedom

**About [[NPCs/Duke Alaric Thorne\|Duke Alaric Thorne]]:**

- Old nobleman who entered the tournament at [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]] (his planned last tournament)
- Crippled right leg
- Has ruled for **eight decades**
- Wife passed away long ago
- Three sons:
    - **[[NPCs/Edran Thorne\|Edran Thorne]]** (35 years old) - Born and raised to inherit the duchy, knighted, always at father's side
    - **[[NPCs/Marek Thorne\|Marek Thorne]]** (24 years old) - Knight of the realm, spent years abroad as a mercenary
    - **[[NPCs/Calen Thorne\|Calen Thorne]]** (16 years old) - **Bastard son** (no other information known)

### Bareknuckle Boxing Tournament

**The Ring:**

- Lower section of tavern contains bareknuckle boxing ring
- Run by **[[NPCs/Marmaduke Porridgepot\|Marmaduke Porridgepot]]**
- Wagers allowed but mainly for bragging rights
- Rules similar to jousting - no unfair advantages (no breath weapons, no class abilities)
- Three attack options: **Jab, Hook, Uppercut**

**Participants:**

- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]], [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]], and [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] entered

### First Match: Elik'r vs Lyria Swiftstep

**Opponent:**

- **[[NPCs/Lyria Swiftstep\|Lyria Swiftstep]]** - 16-year-old Wood Elf girl
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] immediately aware this could only end badly: either ridiculed for losing to a 16-year-old girl OR known as a bully for beating her

**The Fight:**

- [[NPCs/Lyria Swiftstep\|Lyria Swiftstep]] attacked: Jab, Jab, Uppercut
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] responded: Hook to her ear, Jab, Jab to face
- Final Uppercut to chin sent [[NPCs/Lyria Swiftstep\|Lyria Swiftstep]] **crashing through the ring onto a table**
- Crowd shocked at treatment of sixteen-year-old elf girl by massive dragonborn
- (They don't know there's only a 1-year age gap - [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] is 17, weighs 3000lbs, and stands 6'8")

### Second Match: Elik'r vs Elvva

**Winner Stays On:**

- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] challenged [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]

**The Fight:**

- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] hit [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] three times: Jab, Hook, Uppercut
- **CURSE TRIGGERED:** In [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s head, he heard "**It's all my fault!**"
- Took **20 points of psychic damage**
- Entered **Frightened** state
- (As Paladin of Vengeance, he's supposed to be protecting [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]], not harming her)

**Elvva's Response:**

- Ignored the three allowed boxing moves and **elbowed him in the head**!
- Series of misses and counters
- Rolled **Natural 20** for double hit
- More miss/counter exchanges
- Rolled **another Natural 20**!
- Final Uppercut sent [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] following [[NPCs/Lyria Swiftstep\|Lyria Swiftstep]] out of the ring!

- Party checked local quest/notice board after leaving tavern

- Selected the "**Spud Quest**"

- Flyer posted by [[NPCs/Tansy Porridgepot\|Tansy Porridgepot]]

- Payment offered for solving the problem

**Meeting the Farmer:**

- Walked to rear of tavern where potato field located
- Met **[[NPCs/Dodo Porridgepot\|Dodo Porridgepot]]** (the farmer)

**Investigation:**

- Found **tiny, insect-sized bite marks** on potatoes

**The Trail:**

**"GLORY TO THE MOTH QUEEN"**
