# The Bane Watchers Initiation

---
## Summary

The party underwent the Bane Watchers initiation ritual led by [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]], drinking dragon blood mixed with other substances that sent them into a shared prophetic dream. They witnessed visions of past events, including the original war against [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]], the petrification of a massive purple dragonborn, glimpses of party members' backstories, and a mysterious figure on a storm-tossed ship. Upon waking, [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] briefed them on the nature of [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] - an ancient evil that corrupted and nearly destroyed the dwarves and elves before the dragons imprisoned it. Now that it's been released (thanks to the horn incident), the party has officially joined the Bane Watchers organization and can sense the Bane's presence, though currently only as an overwhelming jumble of voices and sensations.

## Key Events

### Bane Watchers Recruitment

- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] arrived at the party's house to collect on his bet with [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]
- Offered all party members the chance to join the Bane Watchers (not just [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]])
- Warned that the initiation ritual could kill them ("small chance of success, massive chance of death")
- All party members agreed to join

### The Initiation Ritual

- [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] poured a thick, dark brownish-red liquid (dragon blood mixed with other substances) from a small keg
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] attempted to use Prestidigitation to flavor the drink, but [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] stopped him: "Hang on a minute, you wuss... Be a man... dragon... thing"
- **Constitution saving throws determined unconscious duration** (higher save = longer sleep = deeper integration):
    - [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]: 4 (slept 4 hours)
    - [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]]: 6 (slept 6 hours total - 2 hours after Odonia)
    - [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]: 13 (slept 13 hours total - 7 hours after ToooAh)
    - [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]: 15 (slept ~15 hours total - couple hours after Elvva)
    - [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]: 20 (rolled 19+1, slept 20 hours total - longest in party)
- All party members collapsed immediately after drinking
- Pattern: Higher Constitution save = longer unconscious period (processing the dragon blood more deeply)

### The Shared Dream Vision

All party members experienced the same prophetic dream, though they didn't initially know it was shared:

**Vision 1 - The Original Party:**

- Saw five figures: [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]], [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]], [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]], a wood elf, and a human

- Witnessed a massive 12-foot purple dragonborn (non-standard color) fighting against dwarves and other races

**Vision 5 - The Purple Dragonborn Statue:**

- Returned to the petrified purple dragonborn

- Young [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]] (identity unknown at the time) standing on palace balcony with an unrecognized man and [[NPCs/High King Everglade\|High King Everglade]] (still alive at this point)

## Important Lore Reveals

### Ancient History

- **First Races:** Dwarves and goliaths were the original races created by the gods
- **The Bane's First Coming:** Corrupted the dwarves, leading to war between dwarves and goliaths
- **Near Extinction:** Both races nearly wiped out (explaining current rarity - [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] is the only goliath they've seen, dwarves are very rare)
- **Dragon Gods:** Multiple dragon gods ruled the world during this time
- **Dragon Alliance:** Dragons bonded together to defeat [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]
- **Dragon Death:** One dragon god died in the war against [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]
- **Imprisonment:** Dragons successfully imprisoned [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]
- **Underground Network:** Ancient dwarven highway system exists beneath the surface

### The Bane

- **Nature:** Pure evil, corrupts everything, sows chaos
- **Many Names:** Called different things by different races/times
- **Current Status:** Released when party blew the horn
- **Detection:** Dragon blood allows sensing its presence
- **Current Location:** Strong presence in [[Locations/Belendar\|Belendar]], also seen in underground roads
- **Bane Watchers:** Ancient organization dedicated to hunting it, now reduced to few small bands

### Dragon Blood Ritual

- **Source:** Blood of dragon god killed in ancient war
- **Mixed Substance:** Not pure blood - combined with other ingredients
- **Effect:** Allows sensing [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]]'s presence
- **Cost:** Can kill participants (Constitution save required)
- **Initial Experience:** Overwhelming jumble of voices and sensations
- **Development:** Sensing ability improves over time
- **Duration:** Permanent once survived

### Purple Dragonborn

- **Size:** 12 feet tall (massive even for dragonborn)
- **Color:** Purple (non-standard - homebrew, no official purple dragonborn exist)
- **Status:** Petrified/imprisoned in stone by magical spell
- **Location:** Large cavern with bright light in ceiling
- **Activity:** Fought against dwarves and other races
- **Current State:** Statue can still speak/scream
- **Connection:** Specifically called to [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] by name
- **Mystery:** Unknown if he's villain or victim

### Political Revelations

- **[[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]]'s Distrust:** Doesn't trust [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]]
- **[[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]]'s Trust:** Only trusts [[NPCs/Emperor Vladier Drakonhart\|Emperor Vladier Drakonhart]] (currently imprisoned)
- **Tournament Purpose:** [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] competing to secure military alliance contract
- **Investigation Network:** [[NPCs/Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian\|Co'narr 'Banewatcher' Hyborian]] has colleagues in [[Locations/Immortal Draconic Empire\|Immortal Draconic Empire]] and [[Locations/Nolunia\|Nolunia]]

- **True Form:** Could transform into wyvern
