## Combat Encounters

### Moth Queen Battle

**Enemies:**
- **[[NPCs/Moth Queen\|Moth Queen]]** (transformed [[NPCs/Oswick Porridgepot\|Oswick Porridgepot]]) - Large creature, main boss
- **Six smaller moths** (1ft wide) - Minions

**Environment:**
- Main cavern with mounds of potatoes and rotting carcasses
- Fluttering and whispering sounds throughout
- Limited space for movement

**Combat Highlights:**

**Round 1-2 (Initial Assault):**
- Party entered main cavern, moths immediately attacked
- Multiple moth minions swarmed [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] (7 piercing damage total)
- One moth bit [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] from underneath armour (9 piercing damage)
- [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] cast Aid (level 1) on three front-line party members (+5 HP to maximum and current HP for 8 hours)
- Later in combat, [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] cast Aid at level 2 (+10 HP to maximum and current HP)
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] readied Fireball, held concentration despite two moth bites (15 piercing damage total, passed two Constitution saves)

**Round 3 (Odonia's Control):**
- [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] cast Ensnaring Strike on Moth Queen (DC 13 Strength save, failed with 12)
- Vines appeared and restrained Moth Queen
- [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] moved forward to stay near [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]

**Round 4 (Elvva's Green Fireball):**
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] released readied Fireball (level 3, 20ft radius)
- **Fireball turned bright green as cast** (Bane corruption manifestation)
- Obliterated two moth minions
- Hit [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] for 20 fire damage (Dexterity save success, half damage)
- Two dying moths released different coloured mists (blue and green) - [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] passed both Constitution saves
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] cast Hex on Moth Queen (disadvantage on Dexterity checks, +1d6 necrotic per hit)
- Attempted to cast Dragon's Breath as bonus action but unable after Fireball

**Round 5 (Odonia's Serpent Chain Attempt):**
- [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] recovered from blindness (Constitution save 18)
- Attempted to use Twin Blades of the Serpent to grapple Moth Queen
- Combat continued with party coordinating attacks

**Round 6-7 (Moth Queen Major Attack & Moth Explosion):**
- Moth Queen cast green mist (necrotic/poison attack):
  - [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]: Failed Constitution save, took **22 necrotic damage + poisoned condition**
  - [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]: Passed Constitution save, took **11 damage + 5 corrosive damage**
- One moth minion exploded in 15ft radius
- Hit [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]], [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]], and itself
- [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]: 8 fire damage (Constitution save 23, half of 16)
- **[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]: Failed Constitution save, took 16 fire damage, knocked unconscious (0 HP)**
- Exploding moth: 16 fire damage (full)
- [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]'s Spiritual Weapon got attack of opportunity as moth exploded

**Final Rounds (Elvva's Agonising Scorcher):**
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] moved to avoid hitting downed [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] with area spell
- Cast Aganising Scorcher (level 3, 30ft line, 5ft wide)
- Moth Queen failed Dexterity save (rolled at disadvantage due to Hex, DC 13)
- Spell started orange, **turned green mid-cast** (Bane corruption)
- Damage rolls: 5 + 5 + 2 = 12, 40, 30 = **30 damage** before Hex
- Added 2 from Hex = **32 total damage to Moth Queen** (approximately 50% of its total HP)
- **Elvva failed Wisdom saving throw (rolled 5) - third failed save, Bane corruption triggered full reflection**
- **Green flames reversed and engulfed [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] - took full 32 points of reflected damage**
- **[[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] knocked unconscious (0 HP) at moment of victory - Moth Queen defeated**
- DM confirmed: "Everyone has something that the Bane has focused on... when you fail it three times, on the third one, there's a chance it could reflect back"

**Party Members Downed:**
- **[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]** - Knocked unconscious by moth minion explosion (failed Constitution save, 16 fire damage)
- **[[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]]** - Knocked unconscious by own reflected spell (32 damage from Agonising Scorcher, third failed Wisdom save triggered full reflection)

**Post-Combat Healing:**
- [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] used Channel Divinity: Preserve Life (30 HP total split between two unconscious party members, 15 HP each to [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] and [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]])
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] revived first ("Ladies first")
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] joked about being pushed back down
- [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] used Lay on Hands on [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] (15 HP)
- [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] cast Cure Wounds on self (level 2, 4d8+2, rolled 25+2=27, brought to 50 HP total)
- [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] cast Cure Wounds on self (level 2, 4d8+2, rolled 25+2=27 total healing)

**Victory:**
- Moth Queen and all minions defeated
- Charred remains of Moth Queen and scattered minion corpses

## Character Development

### Elvva Beltavar - Bane Corruption Pattern Identified

**Trigger Confirmed:**
- Bane corruption activates on failed Wisdom saving throws during spellcasting
- First two failures: Managed to resist reflection
- **Third failure: Spell damage reflected back at full strength**
- Pattern suggests escalating danger with repeated failures
- **DM Confirmation:** "Everyone has something that the Bane has focused on... when you fail it three times, on the third one, there's a chance it could reflect back"

**Session 34 Manifestations:**
1. Held Fireball turned bright green when cast (visual corruption indicator)
2. Held concentration through two moth bites (Constitution saves 19, 25) - first two Wisdom saves passed
3. Agonising Scorcher cast successfully with green flames - **third Wisdom save failed (rolled 5)**
4. Green flames reversed direction and engulfed caster - **32 damage reflected (approximately 50% of Moth Queen's HP)**
5. Knocked unconscious at moment of victory

**Implication:** Elvva's corruption is active and dangerous, triggering during high-level spellcasting. Citadel visit remains critical priority.
