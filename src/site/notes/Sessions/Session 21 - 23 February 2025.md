## The Grand Joust - Day 1

### Tournament Rules

- Knockout format, 3 tilts per match
- **Points:** Torso hit (1), Head hit (2), Unhorsing (5/instant win)
- Heavy/light lances based on strength
- **Rolls:** 2d20 (Power/Speed) + 1d4 (Accuracy)

### Magical Tea Effects (Pre-Tournament)

- **[[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] (18):** Eyes fell out - blind 4 minutes (wore off before joust)
- **[[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] (22):** Potion of Healing upgraded to Greater
- **[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] (76):** Gave [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] advantage on next attack; [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] takes disadvantage
