## Key Events

### Recap - Three Staircases and a Sealed Door

The party find themselves at the foot of the hidden staircase, inside a small square chamber lit entirely by pale green Bane-fire. Three routes lead away from this central point: the staircase they descended (now sealed behind them), a left-hand staircase from which the faint sound of choral singing can be heard - a voice [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] and [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] both feel they recognise - and a right-hand staircase leading to an unknown destination. Ahead stands a large, sealed stone archway, its surface etched with swirling Bane patterns and split by a thick glowing green fissure. The surviving freed knights from the garrison - led by [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] - take up positions guarding the three staircase entrances as a rearguard, while [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] remains with the party.

Insight checks from the whole party (Tessell: 25, ToooAh: 22, [[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]]: 15, [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]: 15) reveal that whilst the knights are visibly bewildered by the chamber, [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] is not. He is calm - far too calm. Pressed by ToooAh (Persuasion with Elik'r's assistance: 19 and 22), he admits that his mentor once found a site almost identical to this one - in the chasms beneath [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]]. He will say little more, other than that a great deal of blood magic was used to open such places.

The party surmise that the left staircase likely leads up into the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]], consistent with the singing audible from above. [[NPCs/Ser Alric Vayne\|Ser Alric Vayne]] can only speculate that the right-hand staircase exits somewhere outside the town. The party decide against splitting up and turn their attention to the sealed door ahead.

### Blood Magic - The Sealed Archway Opens

Investigation of the sealed stone door reveals no keyhole, lever, pressure panel, or visible mechanism of any kind - only swirling carved patterns across its surface. A faint brownish stain is visible around the frame, consistent with old blood. Recalling [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]]'s mention of blood magic, [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] draws his sword across his palm, taking three points of slashing damage, and presses his bloodied hand flat against the door.

The door exhales - a low, airy sigh - and swings fully open.

Beyond it lies a narrow stone corridor, hairline fissures of green Bane-fire crawling along the floor and walls, pulsing with slow rhythmic light. At the far end, the corridor opens into what appears to be a larger antechamber. [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] accompanies the party; the knights remain behind at the guard positions.

---

### The Draining Corridor

As the party advance approximately halfway down the corridor, all of them - including [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] - feel something pulling at them: a cold, draining sensation, as though something is feeding on their vitality. The party are called upon to make Constitution saving throws (Elik'r: 26, Tessell: 24, Odonia: 21, ToooAh: 17).

All four party members pass. Their Bane attunement manifests visibly - each of them briefly emits a faint green glow that seems to force the draining energy away. [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]], having no such attunement, is not so fortunate. The walls themselves appear to reach for him: a spectral green hand materialises and scorches his arm as it grabs hold, dealing necrotic damage before releasing him. He continues forward, shaken but moving.

---

### The Draconic Archway

The corridor ends at a heavy stone archway with no door. Unlike the first, this one is carved with elaborate imagery rather than runes - approximately twenty dragons depicted in relief around the arch, each distinct in form: four-legged and two-legged, winged and wingless, wyrms and wyverns. Their breath weapons vary - fire, ice, lightning, necrotic gas, and a single beam of light. The carvings are not coloured, but the variations are clear from context.

One carving stands apart from the rest: a wingless dragon breathing fire, noticeably more weathered than its companions, as though it has been touched many more times than the others.

[[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]]'s investigation (18) confirms the dragons are all distinct. She reaches out and strokes the weathered wingless dragon. Nothing happens. [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] investigates (21) and observes the same brownish discolouration on the worn carving that was visible on the sealed door.

[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] presses his still-bloodied palm against the weathered dragon. He takes five points of necrotic damage as something pulls at him - and to his eyes alone, a curtain of green fire parts across the archway, leaving a clear corridor beyond. To everyone else, nothing visibly changes.

The party step through one at a time. The archway reacts to each person individually, the carved dragons animating and breathing green fire at each individual passing through. [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]], having already given blood, walks through freely. The others must make Dexterity saving throws to limit the damage (ToooAh: 20 - 3 fire, 3 psychic; Tessell: 19 - 5 fire, 1 psychic; Odonia: 21 - 9 fire, 3 psychic, with Absorb Elements used as a reaction to halve the fire to 5 and store extra fire damage for her next melee strike). [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]], unable to open the archway with full effectiveness alone, jumps to touch the weathered dragon and bleeds on it before walking through - taking damage as he passes but emerging on the far side with a faint green glow briefly visible around him, circling like an ember.

### The Ember Vein Lair - The Communal Area

Beyond the archway lies a small communal room: rough-hewn tables, remnants of cups and basic provisions, the clear signs of a space that is used but not lived in permanently. Four wooden doors lead off this room - two to the left, two to the right - and a larger, more ornate wooden door stands directly ahead. There are no people present, though the area is clearly in regular use.

The party split to investigate the four side rooms simultaneously.

---

### The Four Rooms

**Tessell's Room (Barracks):** A military barracks carved directly into the rock - bunk-like alcoves cut into the walls, rolled bedding, simple wooden storage trunks. Hanging in two places are black and red robes, one tattered and one in better condition. Tessell takes the better-preserved one - a cloak with a deep hood. At a plain central table, a partially burned letter lies near a spent candle. It is written in Draconic. Tessell reads it.

_The letter reads (translated from Draconic):_ "Brother Kaelis - they told me the dreams would be visions of glory. That I would see the skies aflame and hear the voice of our seraph in triumph. That is not what comes. It is quiet. Too quiet. I stand in ash that stretches farther than sight. And beneath it something breathes. Not above, not around. Beneath. Each breath cracks the earth like a cooling glass. The others say this is proof of awakening, but I do not think it wakes. I think it listens. The High Flame of our order insists we continue the kingdom rites beneath the old caldera. He says the seraph stirs when the veins glow the brightest. But I have seen the glow - it pulses like a wound, not a heartbeat. If you are wise, do not descend when they call you. The fire there does not warm, it waits." - _Signed: Serif, Third Ember._

**Odonia's Room (Study):** Shelved alcoves line the walls, holding books, tomes, and codexes of varying ages. Six simple writing desks are arranged facing each other in the centre. Odonia's arcana check (6) is not sufficient to appraise the collection in any depth - but one book is already open on a table, left mid-read. She studies the visible page.

_The open page reads:_ "And thus the seraph was not crowned in flame, but entombed within it. Where it turned its gaze the world did not burn, it remembered burning. The ember veins are not heralds of its rising, but keepers of its slumber. Let no fool mistake the veins for chains. They are wounds, and through them it dreams. Should the veins be sealed, the dream shall end. Oh, should the dream end, the seraph shall wake. And should it wake…" - _The passage ends there._

**Elik'r's Room (Torture Chamber):** A divided room. On one side, a man hangs dead from ceiling manacles - tortured over what a medicine check (12) confirms was several days, wounds of differing ages visible across his body. In the adjacent space, a second set of manacles hangs empty. Female clothing is folded at the back of the empty cell: a nun's habit. The party note in passing that this may have been [[NPCs/Clara\|Clara]]'s cell, and that she was presumably moved - without her clothing - to wherever the ritual was being prepared. A table opposite holds scattered torture implements of poor quality.

Elik'r searches the dead man's satchel and finds a journal. It is the record of a traveller who arrived in [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]] out of curiosity and died for it. Key entries: Day 2 - the writer observes a division between the cathedral's upper and lower congregations, hears the phrase "ember vein" for the first time. Day 3 - locals will not speak of it; offerings are left at a rock face outside town. Day 5 - a robed figure glimpsed at dusk; the cloak appeared to radiate heat, with a strange greenish flame about it. Day 6 - "This is a cult, I swear. And they found me. They are banging at the door. Please, if you read this - come." Day 12 - writing permitted by captors; the writer reports hearing something - not words, but intention - pressing against his mind: "It is vast, it is old, it is reckoning." Final undated entry, in a different hand pressure: "I have made a terrible mistake. I touched the vein. I understood something. Only for a moment. The seraph does not sleep. It is dreaming us."

**ToooAh's Room (Leader's Office):** More ornate than the other rooms - a single desk, a more substantial chair, and the general impression of a commander's workspace, kept tidy and in order. No hidden compartments are found (investigation confirms there are none). In a drawer, amongst writing implements, lies a sealed scroll tied with wax - the seal a cracked flame motif, the insignia of the [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]]. It is unbroken.

ToooAh cannot read it - the language is Draconic. She takes it to Elik'r, who translates.

_The letter reads (translated from Draconic):_ "To the bearer of the third vein - your last report confirms our fears. The western fissure dims. Not from a lack of offering, but from interference. Someone has begun siphoning the heat. This cannot be allowed to continue. You are authorised to enact the Ash Protocol. Silence any outsiders and recover what was taken. If the vein collapses entirely, the balance may shift beyond correction. Understand this clearly - we do not serve the seraph by awakening it. We serve it by keeping the world unworthy of its gaze. Should the veins fail, the sky itself may burn to remind us." - _Signed: The Inner Ember._

**Calen's Find (Journal):** [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] emerges from a side room reading aloud from a book he has recovered - a journal of [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] doctrine. He reads selected entries to the group: Entry 47 - "The veins respond more readily to sacrifice than to prayer. This is expected. Flame is not devotion, it is consumption." Entry 52 - lesser acolytes report auditory hallucinations; whispering. The writer considers this progress rather than madness: "The seraph's cognition bleeds upward through the veins." Entry 61 - three convergences have been mapped beneath the Caldera; when all are ignited in unison, "the ground trembles not with violence, but anticipation." Entry 68 - disagreement among the inner circle: "Some believe we are sustaining the seraph. Others fear we are feeding it. The distinction is irrelevant. Both lead to its return." Entry 72 - "I have begun to dream while awake. I see through the ash. I hear through the flame. It knows we are here. It approves."

The writing across all five documents is confirmed to be from different hands - none of the texts share an author.

### The Ember Veins - Visions of the Seraph

The party reconvene in the communal area and share what they have found. Their attention turns to the glowing green fissures in the corridor walls. [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] announces he intends to touch one.

[[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] begins playing her flute - a soothing melody, she insists, though a Performance check (2) suggests otherwise. It echoes, off-key and rather too loud, down the stone corridors.

[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] presses his hand to the fissure. His white dice roll: 3. What follows, to all external observers, is nothing at all. Elik'r stands very still. To Elik'r alone, in a rapid barrage of overlapping imagery - much of it too fast and fragmentary to process - three images resolve clearly: a vast, wingless dragon, deep in a cavern of embers, turning its head and staring directly at him and saying a single word - _"Release."_ Then: a dragonborn warrior lifting a sword that ignites of its own accord, the blade bright with ember-light. Then: a banner, recognisable to Elik'r as his own family's, fluttering - and then burning.

The vision ends. Elik'r makes a Wisdom saving throw and rolls a Natural 1. He takes 16 points of psychic damage as his mind is overwhelmed. He snaps back to consciousness, breathing hard, to the sound of [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]]'s flute. He uses Lay on Hands on himself, then describes what he saw to the group.

[[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] elects to touch the fissure next. His white dice roll: 1. The same initial vision - the same wingless wyrm, the same ember-lit cavern - but what the wyrm says to Tessell is different. It does not say _"Release."_ It says _"Kill them all."_ The vision then cuts to a crowd of figures in black and red cloaks - like the one now draped over Tessell's arm - and then to a ritual chamber, a stone table, and a woman with a blade raised above her.

Tessell fails his Wisdom saving throw (dirty 20 - not enough), taking 14 points of psychic damage. He uses Healing Word on himself to recover some of the damage. The party discuss the divergent messages: the seraph, it seems, is saying different things to different people.

No one else chooses to touch the fissure.

### The Ritual Chamber - Into the Fight

The large ornate double doors open inward onto a corridor from which the sounds of ritual chanting, shuffling feet, and ceremonial movement can already be heard. A second set of heavy doors stands at the far end. [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] pushes through.

The chamber beyond is large by the standards of this underground complex. Standing at its centre, facing away from the doors, is a man the party have never directly confronted but whose description - and [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]]'s visible recognition - confirms his identity: this is [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]], High Commander of the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]]. He is armoured and carries a heavy mace, its head already flickering with flame. To his side stands a woman in a black and red cloak - not [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]]'s armour, but a flowing garment, her bearing entirely different. Between them, strapped to a stone table, is a young girl with long blonde hair. Alive. It is [[NPCs/Clara\|Clara]].

The chamber is also occupied by a mixed force of acolytes and former Silver Lantern soldiers, all wearing the black and red livery of the [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]]. [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] confirms [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]]'s identity on sight.

**Initiative:** Tessell (Natural 20 + 1 = 21) · ToooAh (15) · Odonia (11) · Elik'r (10).

### Combat - The Rescue of Clara

**Round 1**

[[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] goes first, casting **Spirit Guardians** (spectral, radiant butterflies emanating in a 15-foot radius) and **Shield of Faith** as a bonus action, before advancing deep into the pack of enemies. Three acolytes within range fail their Wisdom saving throws (DC 14) and take 12 radiant damage each. The cloaked woman makes her save, taking only 6.

The woman's response is immediate and unnerving: she ignites - or appears to - and rematerialises in a column of flames at the far end of the chamber, placing herself directly before the back wall.

[[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] darts in and delivers two rapid strikes against the nearest acolyte (9 and 13 damage) before a bonus Unarmed Strike misses on a Natural 1. [[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]], moving with the decisive violence the party have come to expect from him, drives his shield into the nearest acolyte and follows through with a stab to the chest, killing him outright.

[[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] moves to get a line on [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] - and fires. The arrow flies wide of its mark and buries itself squarely in [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]'s shoulder. Her second shot veers into the ceiling. (8 damage, friendly fire.)

[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] marks the acolyte [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] wounded with **Hunter's Mark**, misses his first strike, then drives his blade straight into the man's throat - kill confirmed.

[[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] turns his attention to [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]], striking with his flaming mace twice. The first blow is caught on Tessell's shield; the second breaks through and connects, dealing 10 bludgeoning and 5 fire damage. As a bonus action, [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] slams his mace into the floor and calls _"Cinder to me"_ - the tiles warp and ember-light rises from the stone nearby as a summoned fire entity takes form.

The remaining acolytes press in, a spearman driving through [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s guard for 5 piercing damage.

[[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]'s Spirit Guardians pulse across six enemies in range. He spends his Hero Token to convert the emanation to a critical hit - 42 radiant damage to each. Five acolytes are obliterated outright. [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] is bloodied.

The summoned fire entity erupts from the floor. Despite a Dexterity saving throw of 18 (aided by [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s Paladin aura), Tessell takes 13 fire damage and is left critically low.

**Round 2**

[[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] opens his turn with a bonus action **Healing Word**, recovering 13 hit points, then fires a **Guiding Bolt** at the cloaked woman. The shot slams into a wall of green flame she raises effortlessly - 18 does not meet the AC to hit. His last third-level spell slot is spent.

The cloaked woman turns to face the back wall of the chamber. With a sweep of her arms, the illusion concealing it falls away entirely. The wall disappears, revealing a colossal stone statue: an armoured dragonborn warrior, twelve feet tall, one hand resting on the pommel of a massive longsword pointed downward. Around its base stand four unlit pedestals. Beside the statue a banner hangs in the still underground air - unmistakeably [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s family crest. Elik'r recognises it immediately; no one else does. The woman begins chanting toward the statue. The **first pedestal** erupts in green flame.

[[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] kills the last remaining acolyte and flies to an elevated position.

[[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] uses his full action to pick up [[NPCs/Clara\|Clara]] from the table and run for the stairs.

[[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] repositions and fires twice at [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]]. Both shots miss.

[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] shifts his **Hunter's Mark** to [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] as a bonus action and attacks twice with advantage (Darius is surrounded), dealing 15 total damage.

[[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] retaliates - one strike connects on [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] for 12 total damage, and the return swing at Tessell is caught on his shield. As his main action, [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] drives his mace down and calls a vertical column of fire across a 10-foot radius. [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] (21) and [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] (21) both save and take half damage. [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] fails (12) and takes 14 fire plus 7 radiant - dropping to zero hit points.

**Round 3 - Tessell is down**

[[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]'s turn comes first. He rolls his death saving throw: Natural 1 - two failures simultaneously. He is one failed save from death.

The cloaked woman continues her incantation. The **second pedestal** erupts.

[[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] uses **Wholeness of Body** (action) to restore 18 hit points and readies her staff.

[[NPCs/Brother Calen\|Brother Calen]] carries [[NPCs/Clara\|Clara]] further toward the exit.

[[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] repositions and fires twice at [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]]. The first shot misses. The second - a radiant arrow - strikes him squarely between the eyes. He erupts, flame sheeting off him in a shock wave. [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] (5) and [[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] (11) both fail their Dexterity saves and take 16 fire damage each. [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] (18) saves and takes 8. The **third pedestal** erupts.

[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] casts **Cure Wounds** on [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] at high level (4d8 + 3 = 22 HP), bringing him back to consciousness, then casts **Shield of Faith** on himself as a bonus action (AC 21).

**Round 4**

[[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]] opens with **Channel Divinity: Preserve Life**, distributing 30 hit points - 16 to himself (to half maximum) and 14 to [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]. He uses **Healing Word** as a bonus action on [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] for a further 14 hit points, recovering 4 himself from the spell level. Tessell is no longer bloodied.

The cloaked woman continues her incantation.

[[Party Members/ToooAh\|ToooAh]] swoops down on [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]], spending a ki point on **Stunning Strike** (Constitution save: DC 14 - he meets it; the stun fails to land), but the blow connects regardless. She strikes with her quarterstaff two-handed, driving it down onto his head. [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] crumbles and is dead.

[[Party Members/Lady Odonia of Galantri\|Lady Odonia of Galantri]] repositions and fires twice at the cloaked woman. The first shot misses. As the woman phases momentarily back into solidity, the second connects (25 to hit; 8 total damage). The **fourth and final pedestal** erupts in green flame.

[[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]] charges the cloaked woman and swings twice. His sword passes clean through her both times, as though she is not entirely present in the physical world. She seems barely to notice.

### The Entity Speaks - Session End

The moment the final pedestal ignites, all four party members feel a tremendous tug from somewhere deep beneath the earth - a grinding, massive, slow stirring from far below. The chamber floor begins to tremble.

The cloaked woman lowers her arms. She turns and faces the party. Then, as though from a great distance - a voice carried on warm air, soft and unhurried - she speaks:

_"You carry a most peculiar talent. Twice now I have seen you unravelling that which was never meant to wake. The ancient ones stir because of you - whether by fate, folly, stupidity, or something far older pulling at your strings."_

She tilts her head. Her eyes shimmer with what appears to be genuine concern.

_"But listen well. What is awakened must be returned. Their dreams are not meant for this world, and their waking thoughts less so. You will need all the help you can get to put them back to bed. Now - your first real trial begins."_

She raises her hands and says a single word: _"Arise."_

Behind her, the dragonborn statue - stone a moment before - bursts into flames. The sound of heavy plate armour reverberates through the chamber as it steps down from its plinth: an enormous purple dragonborn warrior, twelve feet tall, armoured, carrying the ember-veined longsword from the statue. Its blade is dark iron shot through with lines of ember-red light, radiating heat, the sound of slow breathing audible from within the metal itself. On its armour, a clasp: [[Party Members/Elik'r Stormswallow\|Elik'r Stormswallow]]'s family crest.

Before the fight can begin, the woman snaps her fingers. [[Party Members/Elvva Beltavar\|Elvva Beltavar]] materialises in the chamber - pulled from the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]] above, present for the first time this session. Simultaneously, each member of the party feels a wave of restoration move through them: wounds close, exhaustion lifts, spell slots return, ki refills. They stand at full strength as though they have taken a full long rest.

The unknown purple dragonborn stands before them, blade in hand.

The session ends here.
