## Tactical Considerations

### If Duke Supports Eagle

**Best Case:**

- Crimson Pike fights for Duke (Eagle ally)
- Party monitors for Falcon bribery attempts
- Keep them at arm's length

**Worst Case:**

- Falcon outbids during battle
- Crimson Pike switches sides mid-conflict
- Disaster scenario

**Mitigation:**

- Don't share critical intelligence
- Don't rely on them for key operations
- Have contingency if they turn

### If Duke Supports Falcon

**Scenario:**

- Crimson Pike becomes enemy
- Professional threat
- Could be bribed to Eagle
- Expensive and unreliable

**Counter-strategy:**

- Don't bribe (waste of gold)
- Treat as hostile professionals
- Defeat militarily instead

### If Duke Stays Neutral

**Scenario:**

- Crimson Pike stays in Neverwinter
- Defensive contract only
- No threat to either side
- Best outcome for everyone
