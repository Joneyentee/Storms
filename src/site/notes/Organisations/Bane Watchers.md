## Initiation Ritual - Dragon Blood

### The Ritual Substance
- Dragon blood from the dragon god killed in First War
- Thick, dark brownish-red liquid
- Mixed with unknown substances
- Served warm from small keg
- Tastes "like shit" (per Co'narr)

### Ritual Process
1. Candidate offered membership (must trust them)
2. Drink the dragon blood mixture
3. Constitution saving throw required
4. **Failure:** Take damage (5+ observed), extended unconsciousness, possible death
5. **Success:** Still unconscious 4-20 hours
6. During unconsciousness: shared prophetic vision
7. Wake with permanent Bane Sense ability

### Risks
- High chance of death
- Even survivors suffer damage
- Extended unconsciousness
- No way to reverse once consumed

### Rewards
- Permanent Bane Sense ability
- Shared prophetic vision (reveals hidden truths)
- Official Bane Watcher membership
- Access to organization's knowledge and resources

**See:** [[General Files/Campaign Mechanics\|Campaign Mechanics]] for party's ritual results (Session 18)
