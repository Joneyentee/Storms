**Stated Goal:** Maintain legitimate royal succession under [[NPCs/Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade\|Crown Princess Eilrora Everglade]]

**Official Position:**
- Crown Princess is legitimate ruler (granddaughter of High King)
- Ser Alvar Flowers is bastard with no claim
- Defending rightful government
- Restoring peace and order to kingdom

**Hidden Truth (Party Knows):**
- [[NPCs/Avicia\|Avicia]] has superior legal claim (rightful heir)

- Three-way succession dispute (Crown Princess, Alvar bastards, Avicia)

- Law and tradition

**Propaganda:**
- Falcon faction = illegitimate bastards and traitors
- Crown Princess = rightful ruler
- Defending kingdom from rebels

**Leadership Style:**
- Crown Princess ruling from throne
- Advisors (Eldrin, Odonia) counsel

- Strategic decision-making
- Willing to elevate capable individuals (Odonia's promotion)

## History & Development

### Formation (Session 23)

**Trigger Event:** High King Everglade assassinated by Thalas

**Civil War Outbreak:**
- Kingdom split into factions
- Eagle (Crown Princess) vs Falcon (Ser Alvar)
- Approximately 50/50 initial split
- Open rebellion throughout Odessia

**Initial Status:**
- Crown Princess not yet on throne
- Eldrin Oakenhart as advisor
- Palace under siege
- Uncertain support levels

### Palace Siege (Sessions 23-25)

**Defense Campaign:**
- Party defended palace ramparts
- Four siege wyverns attacked
- Black Knight led Falcon forces
- Coordinated multi-front assault
- Falcon faction orange/blue mercenaries

**Casualties:**
- High King Everglade (already dead - Thalas)
- Ser Alarin Silverleaf (veteran noble)
- Bulk of veteran commanders
- Many Royal Guards
- Significant leadership losses

**Victory (Session 25-26):**
- Throne room defended
- Thalas/Elincia identity revealed
- Crown Princess saved
- Eldrin Oakenhart survived
- Palace and city secured

### Consolidation (Session 26)

**Crown Princess Takes Throne:**
- First time seated on father's throne
- Royal court reorganized
- Advisors positioned (Eldrin right, Odonia left)
- Government functioning from throne room

**Military Reorganization:**
- General Thailander Oakwood highest ranking
- Riders of Storm commissioned as Marshals
- Military titles distributed
- Command structure rebuilt
- Troop reinforcements allocated

**Honours & Elevation:**
- Lady Odonia elevated to nobility
- Ser Tessell formally knighted
- Party granted military ranks
- Official deed to Stormhold Keep
- War chest access authorized

**Strategic Planning:**
- Rally the North mission assigned
- Neverwinter identified as first target
- Eastern border security discussed
- Civil war strategy developed
