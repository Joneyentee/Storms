## Core Information

**Purpose:** Maintain the slumber of a vast ancient entity called the seraph, entombed beneath an old Caldera, via a network of subterranean ember fissures — the "veins." The [[Organisations/Ember Vein\|Ember Vein]] does not seek to wake the seraph; their stated goal is containment through sacrifice, ritual, and vigilance. Internal doctrine holds that sealing the veins would end the seraph's dream and wake it — the veins are described as wounds through which it dreams.

**Doctrine (from recovered texts):**

- The veins respond to sacrifice rather than prayer — "flame is not devotion, it is consumption"
- Lesser acolytes report auditory hallucinations described as the seraph's cognition bleeding upward through the veins
- Three convergences mapped beneath the Caldera; igniting all three in unison intensifies the seraph's breath
- Internal disagreement exists: some members believe they are sustaining the seraph; others fear they are feeding it — the doctrine holds the distinction irrelevant as both lead to its return
- The seraph is described as "not crowned in flame but entombed within it" — the veins are keepers of its slumber, not heralds of its rising

**Known Ranks:**

- **Inner Ember** — highest confirmed authority; authors the Ash Protocol; identity unknown
- **High Flame** — operational command; insists on continuing rites beneath the Caldera; identity unknown
- **Third Ember / Third Vein** — bearer designation (confirmed [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]]'s rank); authorised to enact Ash Protocol locally
- **Acolytes** — lesser members; subject to auditory hallucinations as "progress"

**Known Protocols:**

- **Ash Protocol** — authorises silencing of outsiders interfering with the ember fissures and recovery of siphoned heat; enacted by bearer of the third vein

**Symbol:** A cracked flame — an ember; used as wax seal on official correspondence

**Uniform:** Black and red cloaks; worn by all confirmed members

## History & Development

**Founding:** Unknown. The underground complex predates the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]], the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]], and likely the town of [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]] itself. Blood magic is integral to the construction — both entry points to the complex require blood to open. A near-identical site exists beneath [[Locations/Neverwinter\|Neverwinter]], suggesting the organisation and its infrastructure predate current political structures across northern [[Locations/Odessia\|Odessia]].

**Pre-Campaign:** The cult operated beneath [[Locations/Thalas'Miriel\|Thalas'Miriel]] without detection. [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] rose through the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]] — originally sent as a young page to the [[Locations/Cathedral of Gentle Redemption\|Cathedral of Gentle Redemption]] — and eventually became High Commander, corrupting the Order to serve as the Ember Vein's surface apparatus. Over the month prior to Session 36, he systematically purged Crown loyalists, shut the garrison gates, and mobilised remaining members toward cult objectives.

**Session 36:** First indirect signs of cult activity — banners in the garrison guard room found defaced with green cult symbol matching the symbol on the corridor wall.

**Session 37:** Green cult symbol on garrison wall activated by [[NPCs/The Bane\|The Bane]] flowing from [[Party Members/Ser Tessell Highfrost\|Ser Tessell Highfrost]]'s palm, opening the hidden passage to the underground complex. Cult connection to [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] and the [[Organisations/Order of the Silver Lantern\|Order of the Silver Lantern]] established.

**Session 38:** [[Organisations/Riders of the Storm\|Riders of the Storm]] entered and explored the [[Locations/Ember Vein Underground Complex\|Ember Vein Underground Complex]]. Five cult documents recovered (letters, journals, doctrine texts). [[NPCs/Darius Kain\|Darius Kain]] killed in ritual chamber. [[NPCs/Ember Vein Envoy - Unnamed\|Ember Vein Envoy - unnamed]] revealed as a figure of considerable power; animated [[NPCs/Purple Dragonborn\|Purple Dragonborn]] and departed. Seraph confirmed stirring; all four pedestals ignited.
