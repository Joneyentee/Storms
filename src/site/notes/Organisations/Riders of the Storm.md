### Tactical Lessons Learned (Session 28)

**Mock Battle Analysis - Decisive Defeat:**

**Critical Failures:**

- Tessell: No assigned troops, completely exposed
- Elik'r: Pushed too far forward, isolated
- Formation gaps: Enemies exploited openings to surround forces

**Key Lesson:** Don't leave openings to be surrounded; maintain formation integrity

**Tactical Weaknesses Exposed:**

- Overconfidence in individual prowess over coordinated tactics
- Insufficient communication between field commanders
- Aggressive charges without adequate support
- Commander positioning needs refinement (Tessell requires assigned troops)

**Training Priorities:** Formation discipline, inter-unit coordination, commander-unit cohesion
